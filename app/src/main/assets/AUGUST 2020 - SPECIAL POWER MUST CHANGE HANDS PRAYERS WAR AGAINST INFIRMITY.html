Praises

Bible Confessions: Psalms 91 and 46

Three songs on the blood of Jesus.

  1. Father, we confess and repent of the sins that opened the door for this pestilence, in the name of Jesus.

  2. We plead for mercy and forgiveness, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O Lord, let Your mercy speak and have mercy upon us as a nation, in Jesus’ name.

  4. O Lord, let Your healing power begin to operate upon our land, in Jesus’ name.

  5. We bind and cast out the crowned dragon from the East out of our land, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Divine immunity, by the blood of Jesus, overshadow all our health workers, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Power of the Most High, overshadow those seeking for cure and prevention, in the name of Jesus.

  8. O Lord, grant our leaders divine wisdom to manage this crisis, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Every wicked spirit, assigned to terminate my life, perish and to melt away like wax in the fire, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Thunder fire of God, scatter, destroy, and break into pieces every dark gathering against my existence, in the nameof Jesus.

  11.    The wall of the blood of Jesus is around me and my household, any evil force that comes near us shall catch fire, inthe name of Jesus.

  12.    Lord, let the power base of this pandemic in the heavenlies and the waters be broken to pieces, in the name of Jesus.16. O great wind from the wilderness, arise, locate the base of this pandemic and blow it away, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Blood of Jesus, laminate everyone in this country and protect them from death, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    As I dwell inside the envelope of divine fire, I become untouchable by the power of infirmity, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    O wind of God, arise and sweep away the scourge from our land, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    By the power in the blood of Jesus, we raise an altar of victory, praise, worship of Jehovah in the land and nations, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    We command the air to vomit all the seeds and tokens of death it has haboured, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Passover blood of Jesus, envelope our nation, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Passover blood of Jesus, envelope our land, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Passover blood of Jesus, envelope me and my family, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    By the power in the blood of Jesus, we stop the spread of this pandemic, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    By the thunder of Your power, O Lord, sweep away the power of this pandemic, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Thou pandemic, hear the word of the Lord, your time is up, die, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Every rage of eaters of flesh and drinkers of blood, we stop you before you stop us, in the name of Jesus.
