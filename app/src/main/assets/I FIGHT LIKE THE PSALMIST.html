Scripture Reading: Psalm 35 

Confession: Psalm 144:1

PRAYER POINTS
  1. Every enemy saying where is my God, be disgraced, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Every power planning to tear my soul like a lion, be dismantled, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every power of the dog working late at night against me, be dismantled, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Every power of the night working against my victory, die, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every organised worker of iniquity, depart from me, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every counsel of evil kings against me, be nullified by the power in the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Every worker of death, be cast down and be unable to rise, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Every drinker of blood and eater of flesh, coming against me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Every cord of darkness, militating against my breakthroughs, die, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Every band of the wicked, that is arresting my progress, break, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Every lying lip speaking against me, be silenced, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    O God, vex my stubborn oppressors in Your sore displeasure, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    O God, arise and laugh at the plot of the wicked fashioned against me, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    O God, arise and swallow up my enemies in Your wrath and let Your fire devour them, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    O God, make my adversaries as a fiery oven in the time of Thine anger, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    O God, arise and speak destruction into the camp of my oppressors, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    O God, arise and laugh my enemies to scorn, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    O God, arise and afflict my affliction, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    O God, arise with all Your weapons of war and fight my battles for me, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    O God, break the teeth of the evil lion targeted at me, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    O God, avenge me and subdue my adversaries under me, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    O gates of brass and bars of iron, working against me, brake, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    O God, arise and speak in great wrath at the enemy of my breakthroughs, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    O God, bring down every high look that is downgrading my potentials, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    O God, thunder from heaven against all my oppressors, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    O God, shoot out Your lightening and discomfit every enemy of my soul, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    O God, wound the heart of all my stubborn pursuers, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    O God send out Your arrows and scatter all my oppressors, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    O God, dash the power of stubborn pursuers in pieces like a potter’s vessel, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    O God, break the head of the dragon in the water, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    O God, break the head of Leviathan in pieces, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    O God, destroy the wicked and put out his name forever and ever, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    O God, visit every power lying against me with destruction, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    O God, ordain Your arrows against my persecutors, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    O God, smite the enemies in their hinder parts and put them to a perpetual reproach, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    O God, prepare the instruments of death against my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  38.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  40.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
