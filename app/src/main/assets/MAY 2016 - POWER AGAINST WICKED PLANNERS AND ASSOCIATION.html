Scripture Reading: Psalm 2

Confession: Isaiah 8:9-10

Aggressive Praise Worship
  1. Every association, anointed to disgrace me, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Every association, anointed to embarrass me, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every association, anointed to mock me, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Every association, anointed to laugh me to scorn, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every association, praying satanic prayer against me, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every association, wishing evil for me, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Every association, planning untimely death for me, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I fire back, every arrow of death and hell, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Every pit, dug for me by the enemy, swallow the enemy, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Every incantation, spell, covens, organized against me, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Every power, planning to put me to shame, wear your garment of shame, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Every pit, dug for me, by the enemy, swallow your digger, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Every intelligent enemy, assigned to pull me down, die, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Anything that the enemy has stolen from me, I recover it seven-fold, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Every coven, decision, judgment and pronouncement upon my life, be crushed, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Any power summoning me from the gate of death, be silenced, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Every voice, speaking against my destiny, shut up by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Holy Ghost fire, melt away every pronouncement assigned to pull me down, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Where is the Lord God of Elijah? Arise judge every tongue condemning me, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    I cancel by the blood of Jesus, every evil wish against me, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    I cancel by the blood of Jesus, every satanic prayer against me, in the name o f Jesus.

  22.    Every utterance, designed to shorten my life, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Power of God, flow into the heavenlies, into the waters and underneath the earth and cancel satanic decisions for my life, inthe name of Jesus.

  24.    Evil pronouncements, from the kingdom of darkness to control and manipulate me, I cancel you by fire, in Jesus’ name.

  25.    Every conspiracy, assigned to cage my destiny, be scattered, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Expectation of darkness, concerning my health, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Expectation of witchcraft powers, concerning my well-being, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    O great wind from the wilderness, arise, destroy the houses of satanic prophets prophesying against me, in Jesus’ name.

  29.    O great wind from the wilderness, arise, muffle the voices of evil powers speaking against my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Every incantation of darkness and death wishes, against me, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Every power of the enemy, having satanic confidence to curse me, be judged and punished, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    All hidden places of boasting Goliath, be exposed and shattered to pieces, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    Every voice, speaking to terminate my existence, be terminated, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    Weapons of warfare, from satanically anointed tongue, assigned against me, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    You the hammer of the Almighty God, smash every evil altar erected against me, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    O Lord, send Your fire to destroy every evil altar fashioned against me, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O thou, that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  38.    Every enemy, of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O God, arise and uproot, anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  40.    Fire of revival, fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
