Praise worship

Scripture Reading - Mark 10

Confession - Psalm 124:7

  1. Every strongman of my father’s house, die, in the name of Jesus

  2. Let the backbone of the stubborn pursuer and strongman break, in the name of Jesus.

  3. I clear my goods from the warehouse of the strongman, in the name of Jesus

  4. I bind every strongman delegated to hinder my progress, in the name of Jesus.

  5. I bind the strongman behind my spiritual blindness and deafness and paralyse his operations in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every inherited covenant affecting my life, break and release me, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Any evil covenant prospering in my family, be broken by the blood of Jesus.

  8. Let my stubborn enemy wake up with confusion, in the name of Jesus.

  9. I scatter any wicked meeting designed against my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Any serpent assigned to swallow my destiny, die, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    I receive uncommon wisdom to excel, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Every gang-up against my peace, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Every programme of failure fashioned against my destiny, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Lord, grant me unquestionable victory, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    My Father, accelerate my speed and close the gap between where I am and where I should be, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Every power assigned to put off my light, receive confusion, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    All destiny robbers, bow, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Every evil eye monitoring my destiny, go blind by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every curse or covenant bearing my name in the heavenlies, break, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    My Father, deliver me from the power of the lions, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Every Python assigned to squeeze out my prayer life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    All foxes assigned to destroy my life, be destroyed, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    My Father, let me dip my feet in Your oil, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    My Father, let my teeth be white with milk, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    My Father, wash my steps with butter, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    I break all curses of premature death, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    O God, recover every dart from my liver, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Any sickness assigned to eat my flesh, die, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Every tumour and any evil growth, melt away by the power in the blood of Jesus.

  30.    Any infection in my body, receive the fire of God and burn to ashes, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    I rebuke all foxes assigned against my prosperity, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    O God, surround my life with songs of deliverance, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    O Lord, let Your angels ascend and descend upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    I loose my neck from all satanic bands, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    Make haste, O Lord, and deliver me, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    Every germ of sickness that touches my body, die, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O Lord, give me a sound heart, in the name of Jesus.

  38.    Power of evil pain, die, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  40.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in Jesus’ name.

  41.    O God arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries.

  42.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
