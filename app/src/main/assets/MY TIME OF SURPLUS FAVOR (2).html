PRAISE WORSHIP:

SCRIPTURE READING – Esther 2

CONFESSION: Psalm 144:7 says, “Send thine hand from above; rid me, and deliver me out of great waters, from the hand of strange children;”

PRAYER POINTS

  1. Let those who do not know me fight for my cause, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Every ancestral chain holding down my progress, die, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every prophecy of darkness on my destiny, die, in Jesus' name.

  4. Any power that wants me to die in this condition, die, in Jesus name.

  5. Every witchcraft tree fashioned against my destiny, die, in the name Jesus.

  6. Bitter water flowing from my father' house, dry up and die, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Yoke of stagnancy, break, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Any power that will not let me go when God says go, die, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Thou blood sucking power, die, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Every altar of sleeping and prayerlessness, be overthrown, in the name Jesus.

  11.    Every sleep of affliction, die, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Every anti-prayer power, fall down and die, in Jesus' name.

  13.    Nobody should say to me, 'sorry, better luck next time', in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Every power of the idol of my father's house, die, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Every evil power pursuing me from my father's house, die, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Every evil power pursuing me from my mother's house, die, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Every foundational witchcraft release me, in Jesus' name.

  18.    Every witchcraft tree binding my placenta, die, in Jesus' name.

  19.    Where is the God of Elijah, arise and fight for me, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Every destiny-demoting dream, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Every foundational bondage, break, in the name of Jesus.
