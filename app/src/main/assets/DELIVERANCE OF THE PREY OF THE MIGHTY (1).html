DELIVERANCE OF THE PREY OF THE MIGHTY (1)
(Isa.49:25-26)

PRAISE WORSHIP

SCRIPTURE READING - 1 Chron. 4:9-10

CONFESSIONS - Psalm 71:21

These are destiny changing prayers.There are ten reasons why you should pray them with holy madness.

  1. God has a plan for your life.

  2. Right now you are either in that plan or out of it.

  3. You are not an experiment or an accident.

  4. God knew who you were before your mother and father ever brought you into the world.

  5. If you do not take hold of your destiny, guard and protect it, you will loose it.

  6. There is a conspiracy and battle going on to steal destinies.

  7. There is a department in the dark world that destroys destinies.

  8. When vision is lost, people do not pursue their destinies.

  9. When there is no vision, there is no destiny.

  10.    To every man or woman of destiny, there is a conspiracy in the heavenlies.

PRAYER POINTS:
  1. I come against destiny killers by the resurrection power of the Lord Jesus Christ, in the name of Jesus.

  2. I enforce and effect God’s original plan and purpose for my life, in the name of Jesus.

  3. I decree that no spiritual and organizational weapon formed against my destiny shall prosper, in Jesus name.

  4. I pull down the strongholds and cast down the imaginations working against my breakthroughs, in Jesus name.

  5. Anointing that breaks the yoke, break every yoke over my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  6. I establish barricades against the enemies of my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  7. I overrule every satanic sanction that opposes my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I dispossess spiritual destiny robbers, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Let the angels of fire handle any satanic contentions against my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Let terror strike the hearts of the enemies of my destiny and cause their hearts to fail, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    My Father, contend with those who contend against my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    My Father, arise with Your weapons of war and overthrow the chariots, horses and riders militating against my destiny.

  13.    O Lord, establish my name in the heavens and let strangers submit themselves to me, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    My Father, impose my divine destiny over all satanic contentions, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    I bind every satanic surveillance over my life, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    I dismantle every satanic harassment targeted against my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    My life, open to divine deliverance, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    My genes, receive deliverance from inherited problems, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every power of my father’s house, acting as a doorkeeper to my soul, die, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Blood of Jesus, make it impossible for the enemy to track down my star in the realms of the spirit, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    My Father, expose my masquerading enemies by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    My Father, empower me to increase in skill and understanding, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    All visible and invisible walls against my destiny, break, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    I execute divine judgment against every demonic activity militating against my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    I operate according to God’s divine timetable and calendar, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    I decree that God’s agenda is my agenda, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Every witchcraft prayer targeted against my destiny, die, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    My Father, cast forth lightening to scatter every enemy of my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    I decree that nobility and greatness is my portion, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Every satanic intelligence, working against my destiny, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Every good thing prepared for me before the foundation of the earth, locate me, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Thou power of darkness, cough out and vomit my virtues, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    The sons of strangers shall build up my wall and kings shall minister unto me, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    The timetable of my favor shall not be frustrated, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    We cover the coming MFM convention with the blood of Jesus and we pray that the Lord will manifest His power, in Jesus’ name.
