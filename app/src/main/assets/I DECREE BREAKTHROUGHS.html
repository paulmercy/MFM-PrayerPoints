Scripture Reading: Lamentation 3
Confession: Psalm 16:6

PRAYER POINTS

  1. Thank God for His unfailing love.

  2. The Lord should open up the pipeline of prosperity into my handiwork and my business in the name of Jesus.

  3. I bind every spirit of financial failure in the name of Jesus.

  4. Lord, loose the spirit of prosperity upon me in the name of Jesus.

  5. O Lord anoint me with the power to prosper in the name of Jesus.

  6. Lord, give me breakthrough of blessings in the spiritual, financial, marital and business life in Jesus name.

  7. I declare this year a year of super abundance for me in the name of Jesus.

  8. Let the Holy Spirit take perfect control of my tongue in the name of Jesus.

  9. Lord, give me the grace to express Your love to the needy in the name of Jesus.

  10.   I command every contrary tree growing in my life to be uprooted and thrown into the fire in Jesus name.

  11.   O Lord, rain abundant blessings upon me in Jesus name.

  12.   Lord, make this program the beginning of great blessings in the name of Jesus.

  13.   From the North, South, East and the West, let all my blessings be released and let them come unto me in Jesus name.

  14.   I decree that my stolen blessings should be restored seven-folds in Jesus name.

  15.   Let every evil attachment to my place of birth be disrupted in the name of Jesus.

  16.   I command every agent acting against my breakthroughs to be permanently paralyzed in the name of Jesus.

  17.   I command every power barking against my progress to be silenced in the name of Jesus.

  18.   I receive the anointing for supernatural breakthroughs in all my endeavors in the name of Jesus.

  19.   I receive the anointing of the beautiful feet and the prospering hand in the name of Jesus.
