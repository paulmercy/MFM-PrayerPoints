Scripture Reading: Ezekiel 8

Confession: Psalm 121

AGGRESSIVE PRAISE WORSHIP
  1. O God arise, and uproot anything You did not plant, inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  2. O God, let the fire of revival, fall upon the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O God, let the power of peace and progress, overshadow this nation, in the name of Jesus.

  4. O God, arise and give us God-fearing leaders, in Jesus’ name.

  5. I arrest any serpent of infirmity troubling my body, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Destructive infirmities, hear the word of the Lord, die, in the name of Jesus.

  7. I cut off the tentacles of internal disease spreading in my body, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Power base of infirmity, dry up and die, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Health arresters, be arrested by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   I kill every killer disease by the power in the blood of Jesus.

  11.   The battle of the terrible and the mighty against my health, expire, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   Father, let my bodily organs become too hot for any disease to handle, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   Blood of Jesus, sanitize my body and make me whole, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   Eaters of flesh assigned against me, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   My flesh, my blood, reject the voice of death, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   Any power feeding on my flesh, come out and die, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   Agents of killer infirmities, I kill you now, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   Any power assigned to eat me up, die, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   Every demon termite eating my body, die by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   Every witchcraft poison in my body, dry up and die, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   Blood of Jesus, pump out any stranger in my body, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   I break the yoke of the invisible destroyers, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   Every venom of the serpent and scorpion eating up my flesh, dry up now, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   I drink the blood of Jesus. (Say this for 21 times)

  25.   Bewitchment of my flesh, blood and bones, terminate, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   Wasting powers, depart from my life, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   Any power, tabling my affairs, in a coven, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   Every thought and imagination of darkness against my existence, vanish, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   Every sorcerer and enchanter organized against my existence, destroy yourself, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   Eaters of flesh and drinkers of blood, I am not your victim, drink your own blood and eat your own flesh, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   Every sword of darkness, raise up against me, kill your owner, after the other of Goliath, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   Every destructive incantation, uttered to harm me, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   Every astral projection, fashioned against my existence, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   Every satanic oracle, consulted against me, turn against your owner, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   Spirit of Goliath, spirit of Herod, spirit of Pharaoh, release my body, in the name of Jesus.

  36.   Every coven decision, judgment and pronouncement upon me, be crushed, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   Any power summoning me from the gate of death, be silenced, in the name of Jesus.

  38.   Every voice speaking against my destiny, shut up by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  39.   I withdraw my name from every evil altar, in the name of Jesus.

  40.   Every incantation of darkness and death wishes against me, backfire, in the name of Jesus.
