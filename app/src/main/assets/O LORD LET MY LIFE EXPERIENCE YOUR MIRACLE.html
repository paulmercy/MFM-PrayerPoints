Text: Luke 5:1-8, John 5:1-9

Confession: Eph.3:20

PRAYER POINTS:
  1. Thank the Lord from the dept of your heart.

  2. I reject every journey to backwardness in Jesus name.

  3. I break the curse of unfruitfulness upon my life in Jesus name.

  4. Oh Lord, give me divine direction to greatness in Jesus’ name.

  5. Oh Lord, by your power, I possess the excellence spirit of God in Jesus name.

  6. The power that raised Lazarus from the dead work miracles upon my life now, in Jesus name.

  7. Every spiritual devourer working against me be destroyed, in Jesus name.

  8. Let my spirit, soul and body reject evil loads in the name of Jesus.

  9. Let every area of my life become too hot for the enemy to handle, in Jesus’ name.

  10.    Blood of Jesus, reverse every arrangement of poverty working against my life, in Jesus’ name.
