Praise worship

Scripture Reading - Psalm 2:4; 71:21; Eph. 3:20

  1. O Lord, connect me with the people that matter this year, in the name of Jesus.

  2. O God, arise and order my steps this year, in the name of Jesus.

  3. My legs, hear the word of the Lord, carry me to the place of my destiny this year, in the name of Jesus.

  4. This year, I will get to the right position, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Father, redeem me from the power of death, doom and disaster, in the name of Jesus.

  6. God will bless them that bless me and curse them that curse me this year, in the name of Jesus.

  7. This year, the storm of life will not sweep me away, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I declare that all my journeys this year shall be safe, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Holy Ghost, arise and service every part of my body for strength and health, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   Preserve my life, O Lord, from all sicknesses this year and all the days of my life, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   O God, arise and give me divine power to produce wealth this year, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   This year, I shall not fall from grace, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   O Lord, I shall be surrounded by Your wall of fire throughout this year, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   Every financial grave dug for me this year, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   Every cloud of evil over my life, I blow you away, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   Let the angels of God be installed as the gate-keepers of my habitation, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   This year, satanic nightmare will not harm me, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   This year, every enemy of my life will stumble and fall, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   No divination nor enchantment from evil men will work against my life and family, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   Let every lost opportunity begin to come my way this year, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   Weeping and oppression of the night, expire today, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   I command my portion to be released this year, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   Decision and judgement of the council of hell against my life, I terminate you now, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   I receive my daily increase and enlargement this year, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   Darkness shall have no portion in my life this year, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   O God, arise and pull me out of every pit of evil foundation, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   Seven spirits of God (Isa. 11:2), manifest in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   Let my killer begin to kill himself right now, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   Every trap prepared for me by the enemy, turn around and catch your owner, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   I command the baptism of madness and backwardness to fall upon my stubborn enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   My testimony will not be buried, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   I will not suffer what my parents suffered, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   Seed of untimely death, die, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   Seed of greatness in my life, manifest, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   This year, wealth and riches shall be in my house, in the name of Jesus.

  36.   This year, I shall have no fear of bad news, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   This year, I shall triumph over all evil forces, in the name of Jesus.

  38.   This year, my life shall be a reference point for success and breakthroughs, in the name of Jesus.

  39.   O God arise and arrest the arrester of my prosperity, in the name of Jesus.

  40.   O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  41.   Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in Jesus’ name.

  42.   O God arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries.

  43.   Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
