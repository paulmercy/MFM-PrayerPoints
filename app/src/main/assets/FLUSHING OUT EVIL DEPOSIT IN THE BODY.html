PRAISE AND WORSHIP

CONFESSION: Jer: 32:27, Mark 10:27, Luke 1:37

PRAYER POINTS:
  1. I command every satanic injection in my body be flush out by the blood of Jesus.

  2. Every internal disorder, receive life in the name of Jesus.

  3. Holy Ghost fire, destroy every stubborn agent of disease hiding in my body in Jesus name.

  4. Holy Ghost fire do your surgical operation inside my body in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every death sentence passed against my life, Lord cancel it, in the name of Jesus.

  6. I remove my name from the register of sudden death in the name of Jesus.

  7. I command my digestive system to reject every evil treatment in my dream in Jesus name.

  8. I vomit and pass out any strange deposit in my stomach in Jesus name.

  9. Every covenant of death programmed into my soul break and released me in Jesus name.

  10.   Blood of Jesus purge evil out of my blood.

  11.   Every vow to terminate my life backfire.

  12.   You agent of death assigned to kill me, release me and kill your sender in Jesus name.

  13.   Spiritual poison in my soul, spirit and body, dry up in Jesus name.

  14.   Every strangers in my body, come all the way out of your hiding places.

  15.   Lay one hand on your head and the other on your stomach and begin to pray like this: Holy Ghost fire; burn from the top of my head to the sole of my feet: mention every organ of your body.

  16.   Let all negative materials circulating in my soul, bone, be evacuated in the name of Jesus.

  17.   0 Lord do a total purification in my body in the name of Jesus.

  18.   Blood of Jesus close the gate of death Opened for me by my enemies.

  19.   O Lord return death sentence to my enemy in the name of Jesus.
