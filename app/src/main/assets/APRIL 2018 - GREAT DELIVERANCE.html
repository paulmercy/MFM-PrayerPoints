Scripture Reading: 1 Samuel 17

Confession: Luke 4:18

AGGRESSIVE PRAISE WORSHIP
  1. O God arise, and uproot anything You did not plant, inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  2. O God, let the fire of revival, fall upon the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O God, let the power of peace and progress, overshadow this nation, in the name of Jesus.

  4. O God, arise and give us God-fearing leaders, in Jesus’ name.

  5. Every plantation of failure in my foundation, die, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every negative power, flowing from my foundation, to kill my destiny, die, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Every dark foundational power, troubling my star, die, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I declare war on every enchantment targeted at my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  9. By the power in the blood of Jesus, I command the confidence of the wicked to break by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Every serpent and scorpion of affliction, targeted at my destiny, die, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Every wicked plantation in the garden of my life, be uprooted by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    O God, arise and laugh at the plot of the wicked fashioned against me, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    O Lord, make haste to help me, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    O Lord, let Your arrows pursue and locate every wicked power targeted against me, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    O gates blocking my blessings, be lifted up, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    O God, arise and make all my pursuers turn back, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    O God, arise and swallow up my enemies in Your wrath and let Your fire devour them, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    O Lord, break my enemies with Your rod of iron, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Blood poured on the ground will not eat me up, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    My portion is not with the dead, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Every medicine poured on the ground to subdue my life, I destroy you, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    I destroy the hand of every witchdoctor working against me, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    I quench every anger energised through the land against me, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    I break the law of death over my life, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Every covenant of death with this year, be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Every agent of death buried inside my body, come out and die, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Every stronghold of death on my mind and imagination, be pulled down, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    By the resurrection of the Lord Jesus Christ, the power of death is broken upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    O Rock of Ages, smash into pieces every foundation of witchcraft in my family, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Witchcraft powers, eat your own flesh and drink your own blood, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Every witchcraft padlock, fashioned against me, break by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Every witchcraft identification mark, be wiped off by the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    O gates of brass and bars of iron working against me, be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    O God, arise and speak in great wrath against the enemy of my breakthroughs, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    O God, bring down every high look that is downgrading my potentials, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    O God, deliver me from my strong enemy which hated me for they are too strong for me, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O God, shoot at my enemies with an arrow, and let them be wounded suddenly, in the name of Jesus.

  38.    O God, smite my enemies by the cheekbones, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O Lord, at the blast of Your nostrils, disgrace every foundational bondage in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  40.    O God, thunder from heaven against all my oppressors, in the name of Jesus.
