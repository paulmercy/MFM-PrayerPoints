Scripture Reading: Psalm 17

Confession: Psalm 92:10

Aggressive Praise Worship
  1. I shake off, bullets of darkness, from my head, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Every power using my hair against me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Invisible loads of darkness, upon my head, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Any problem brought to my life through head attacks, die, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Powers of my father’s house, untie my head by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Serpents and scorpions, assigned against my head, die, in the name of Jesus.

  7. I reject the spirit of the tail and I claim the spirit of the head, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I cancel the power of all curses upon my head, in the name of Jesus.

  9. My head, reject the covenant of failure, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   My head (lay your right hand on your forehead), from now on, life shall be easy for you. You shall be desired, appreciated and rewarded, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   Holy Ghost fire, incubate my brain, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   My head, reject every manipulation and bewitchment of untimely death, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   Holy Ghost, crown my head with divine glory, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   I release my head from every evil blood covenant, in the name of Jesus.

    15.  
  15.   Every power that has formed any evil cloud over my head, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   Any evil umbrella covering my head, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   Holy Ghost, hook my head unto my divine destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   Fire of God, consume every strange satanic material in my head, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   O Lord, anoint my head with Your oil, bless my water and bread to eat of the fat of this land, in Jesus’ name.

  20.   Every evil head raised to suppress me, I pull you down by the power of the God of Elijah, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   Blood of Jesus, speak life into my head, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   Any power calling for my head before evil mirrors, die with the mirror, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   My glory, my head, arise and shine, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   Any power calling my head for evil, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   I fire back, every arrow of witchcraft fired at my head, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   I use the blood of Jesus to cancel the effects of evil hands laid upon my head when I was a little child, die, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   My head, reject demotion, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   Arrows of darkness fired into my brain, die, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   Anointing for uncommon success, fall upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   My brain, wake up by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   Any power calling my head for evil, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   Strongholds in my life battling my destiny, I pull you down, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   Anointing to excel, incubate my life now, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   Creative power of God, arise and manifest in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   Every power using my head against me, bow, in the name of Jesus.

  36.   Anointing for excellence, come upon my head by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  38.   Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  39.   O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  40.   Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
