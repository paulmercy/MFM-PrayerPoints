SCRIPTURE READING: 2 Kings 2:18-22

CONFESSION: Numbers 23:23 

“Surely there is not enchantment against Jacob, neither is there any divination against Israel: according to this time it shall be said of Jacob and Israel, What hath God wrought!”

PRAYER POINTS:
  1. I take authority over every curse upon my life in the name of Jesus.

  2. I command all curses issued against me to broken, in the name of Jesus.

  3. I command all evil spirits associated with any curse to leave me now in the name of Jesus.

  4. I take authority over inherited curses and command them to be broken now in the name of Jesus.

  5. I take authority over any curse emanating from my past religion and command them to be broken now in the name of Jesus.

  6. I break any curse which may be in my family back to ten generations in both sides of the family, in the name of Jesus.

  7. I renounce and break all curses put on my family line and my descendants, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I command every bad spirit of any curse to release and let me go, in the name of Jesus.

  9. I break every curse of automatic failure working in my family, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    I take authority over every curses of (sickness, infirmity, deformity) in my family, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    I take authority over every unconscious curse issued on me, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Let the root of my life be purged by the fire of the Holy Spirit, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Let the root of my life be washed in the blood of Jesus.

  14.    I break and cancel every curse placed on children to punish parents in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    I break and cancel every curse placed on me out of jealousy, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    I break and cancel every clinical and medical curse, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    I break and cancel every curse issued by satanic ministers, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    I break and cancel every curse emanating from evil prophecies, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    I release myself from any curse emanating from breaking a person, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Holy Ghost Fire, burn to ashes every satanic sacrifice against me, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Any evil barricade against my promotion be roasted by fire, in the name of Jesus.
