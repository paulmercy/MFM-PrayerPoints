Scripture Reading: Psalm 46

Confession: 3 John 1:2

Aggressive Praise Worship
  1. I thank You, God for making provisions for my prosperity, in the name of Jesus.

  2. I break the curse of poverty in my life, in the mighty name of Jesus.

  3. Every spirit drinking the blood of my blessing, I bind you, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Legs of poverty, walk out of my life now, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Yokes of poverty, break, in the name of Jesus.

  6. O Lord, let the spirit of favour baptise my business and handi-work, in the name of Jesus.

  7. O Lord, enlarge my coast by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I rebuke every devourer in my finances, in the name of Jesus.

  9. O Lord, surprise me with abundance in my finances.

  10.    The anointing for money-yielding ideas, fall upon my life now, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    I withdraw my wealth, from the hand of the bondwoman and her children, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    O Lord, let the riches of the gentiles be transferred to me, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    O Lord, let every coffin constructed for my prosperity swallow the owner, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    I bind the spirit of debt; I shall not borrow to eat, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Every evil meeting, summoned against my prosperity, scatter without repair, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    O Lord, lead me to those who will bless me.

  17.    O Lord, let every design of poverty I inherited melt away by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    I remove my name from the book of financial embarrassment, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    O Lord, let divine magnets of prosperity be planted in my hands, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    I refuse to lock the door of blessings against myself, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    O Lord, let the angels of the Lord pursue every enemy of my prosperity to destruction, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Every demonic scarcity, be dissolved by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    O Lord, let all my departed glory be restored, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    O Lord, let all my departed virtues be restored, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Anyone occupying my seat of prosperity, clear away, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    O Lord, let the riches of the ungodly be transferred into my hands, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    I will rise above the unbelievers around me, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    O Lord, make me a reference point of divine blessings, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Every curse, pronounced against my source of income, break, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    O Lord, network me with divine helpers, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Every power, shielding me away from breakthroughs, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Every Achan in the camp of my breakthroughs, be exposed and be disgraced, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    I break every cycle of financial turbulence, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    Every strange fire ignited for my prosperity, quench, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    O Lord, let those sending my money to spiritual mortuary fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    Every power scaring away my prosperity, be paralysed, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O thou, that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  38.    Every enemy, of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O God, arise and uproot, anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  40.    Fire of revival, fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
