Scripture Reading: 1 Samuel 17

Confession: Isaiah 40:31

AGGRESSIVE PRAISE WORSHIP
  1. O God arise, and uproot anything You did not plant, inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus name.

  2. O God, let the fire of revival, fall upon the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O God, let the power of peace and progress, overshadow this nation, in the name of Jesus.

  4. O God, arise and give us God-fearing leaders, in Jesus name.

  5. Powers, attacking my business/career, die, in Jesus name.

  6. Blessings that swallow poverty, overshadow my life, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Power, assigned to kill the glory of my work, die, in the name of Jesus .

  8. Angels of multiplication, visit the work of my hands, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Spirit of dryness, get out of my work, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   Spirit of dryness, get out of my progress, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   Spirit of dryness, get out of my business/career, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   Powers, assigned to make me move from problems to problems, die, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   King of glory, arise, change my story to glory, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   I wear the garment of power and glory, by the power in the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   Glory of God, illuminate my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   Ark of glory, come into my tarbanacle, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   Satanic stone, blocking my glory, clear away, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   The glory of my hands, the glory of my legs, and the glory of my head, in captivity, receive deliverance, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   Every power attacking me with the wind of the night, I bury you now, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   Power of darkness, assigned to turn me to a beggar, die, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   Wicked spirits, collecting money from me in the spirit realm, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   Every dark power house, assigned against me, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   Every power telling me my life is finished, die, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   Every battle, assigned to give me a bad name, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   Father, at this month power must change hands programme, speak deeply to my foundation, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   Father, today, answer Your name in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   Father, today, as I call upon You, let me see my Egyptians no more, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   Wherever the enemies of my soul shall run to, Father, locate them and bring them down, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   Every problem assigned to disgrace me, be disgraced and die, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   Father, let the covenant in the blood of Jesus speak for me today, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   O hand of G0d, arise, keep me ahead of my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   By the anointing, O Lord, lift away my siege and break the yokes of the enemy, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   I call judgment against everything that is issuing judgment against my life, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   My enemies shall hear a rumour, and the fear of that rumour shall smite them, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   O hand of God, arise, re-write my story and restore my glory, in the name of Jesus.

  36.   Every dark veil upon my life and glory, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   Every enemy trampling upon my Jerusalem, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  38.   Hackers of the Lord, defend my house, in the name of Jesus.

  39.   Any power that has stolen my days of visitation, release them, in the name of Jesus.

  40.   Thou prophetic power, that operated in the valley of dry bones, connect me to the glory of my life, in Jesus name.
