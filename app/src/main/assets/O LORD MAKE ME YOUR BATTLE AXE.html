Praise worship

Scripture Reading – Acts 2

Confession – Isaiah 58:9
  1. Let every power of the oppressors rise up against them in the name of Jesus.

  2. O Lord, lift away the altar of evil inheritance, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O Lord, grant me the power to be fulfilled, successful and prosperous in life in the name of Jesus.

  4. I break satanic powers in the heavenly places, on land, and in the waters, in the name of Jesus.

  5. I refuse to wear the garment of sorrow in the name of Jesus.

  6. Let every power of the oppressor rise up against each other in the name of Jesus.

  7. O Lord, let all the impossible begin to become possible for me in every department of my life in Jesus name.

  8. Let the joy of the enemy upon the problems of my life be turned to sorrow in the name of Jesus.

  9. Let all my blessings that the enemy has swallowed be vomited now in the name of Jesus.

  10.    I reject every spirit that fails at the edge of breakthroughs in the name of Jesus.

  11.    All anti – progressive forces, I summon you together and I issue the judgment of the fire of God in the name of Jesus.

  12.    I reject nightmare and sudden destruction, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    I pull down every stronghold in and over me, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    I reject physical and spiritual accidents in my going in and coming out, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    I bind all evil spirits that oppress me and remove them from my life in the name of Jesus.

  16.    I cast out the spirit of evil inheritance in the name of Jesus.

  17.    I bind every stubborn spirit working me up to the second heaven in the name of Jesus.

  18.    I forsake all personal sins that have given ground to the enemy in my life in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Desolation of my parents you shall not destroy me in the name of Jesus.

  20.    I decree that there shall be no reinforcement, no re-grouping of any strongman against me in the name of Jesus.

  21.    I order confusion and scattering of tongues, amongst all wicked associations militating against the peace of my life in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in Jesus’ name.
