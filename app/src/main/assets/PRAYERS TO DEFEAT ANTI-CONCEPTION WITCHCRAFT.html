PRAISE AND WORSHIP

CONFESSION: Ps 35:1-8

Prayer points
  1. Every witchcraft power hindering my conception be arrested in the name of Jesus.

  2. According to the word of God in Exodus 23:25-26, I shall not be barren in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every evil power and satanic conspiracy against my conception scatter and die in the name of Jesus.

  4. Every satanic hand against my ability to have children be cut of in Jesus name.

  5. Every witchcraft seed inside my reproductive organ die in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every power manipulating my menstrual cycle lose your hold in the name of Jesus.

  7. Satanic heat in my womb and waist die by the blood of Jesus.

  8. Witchcraft spirits of barreness of my father’s house release me and die in the name of Jesus.

  9. Powers monitoring the success of my conception be arrested and die in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Witchcraft spirit assigned to prevent pregnancy in my life in the name of Jesus.

  11.    You power in charge of water loose your power over my child bearing In the name of Jesus.

  12.    Every evil vow from the evil power of my father’s house against my child bearing be revoked by the blood of Jesus.

  13.    Every power responsible for my miscarriage in my life, be arrested in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Any record of barrenness containing my name be cancelled by the blood of Jesus.

  15.    Begin to thank God for answering your prayer.
