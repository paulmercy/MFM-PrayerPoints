Scripture Reading: Matthew 2

Confession: Isaiah 60:1

Aggressive Praise Worship
  1. I use the weapon of the fire of God to clear every blockage in my way, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Let the power in the blood of Jesus Christ laminate my life and protect me from all harms, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O lightening thunder of God, arise, strike terror and fear into the camp of my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  4. O God arise by the weapon of Your lightening and thunder and scatter every conspiracy against my life, in Jesus’ name.

  5. Let the water from the side of Christ wash away every poison in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  6. O lightening and thunder of God arise, go into the waters, go into the forest and go into the mountains and destroy every stronghold mounted against me, in the name of Jesus.

  7. I take authority over all enchantments and divination and evil wishes that have been sent against me, in the name of Jesus.

  8. O lightening and thunder of God, move in your fire and in your rage and pursue my pursuers, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Holy Ghost fire, banish all the curses of darkness from me and destroy them, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Holy Ghost fire, banish all witchcraft and demonic assignments from me, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    I take authority over all enchantments and divination and evil wishes that have passed down my generational blood line, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Hail stones form heaven, arise and disgrace every activity of serpent and scorpion fashioned against me, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Oh sword of God, move forth on my behalf and cut down the citadel of the wicked assigned against my life, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    I break the influence of curses over my life by the power of the risen Lord Jesus.

  15.    By the sword of God, I arise and declare war against my oppressors, in the name of Jesus

  16.    By the sword of God, I arise and destroy every satanic manipulator working against my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    All curses harassing my life, go back to where you come from and be replaced with blessings, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    O sword of the Lord, pass through the camp of my oppressors for destruction, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    I cancel all negative inner vows and agreements that I have made with the enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    My Father, send the horses and its riders assigned to trouble me into deep sleep, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    I release myself from the spirit of death and darkness, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    O deep sleep of the Lord, fall upon every violent warfare targeted against me, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    I release myself from any bondage of enchantment and divination, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    My Father, let my stubborn enemies sleep the sleep of death, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Oh deep sleep from the Lord, arise, trouble the troublers of my Israel, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Oh God arise and send Your wasters to waste every enemy of my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    I claim the shed blood of Jesus over all aspects of my life, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Wasters from heaven, waste every force of Goliath assigned against me, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    I claim angelic protection over all aspects of my life, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Let the wasters of God waste all the wasters assigned against me, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Holy Ghost fire, purify me, mold me, and use me, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Every power assigned to molest me, I command you to leave me forever and be consigned to everlasting lake of fire so that they will not touch me again, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    I renounce every inherited work of darkness in my family tree, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    Lord Jesus, enter into my heart and create in me, the kind of person You intended me to be, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  36.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  38.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
