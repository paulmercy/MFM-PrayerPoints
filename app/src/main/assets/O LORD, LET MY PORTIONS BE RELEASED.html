Scripture Reading - Psalm 3

Confession - Psalm 138:8

PRAYER POINTS
  1. O Lord, repair my foundation to carry uncommon breakthroughs, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Any power boasting against my God, O God arise, and put them off, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every evil handwriting against me, be cut off for ever, in the name of Jesus.

  4. No enchantment shall swallow me, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Everyone ordained for my blessings, appear, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every infirmity, be swallowed up, in the name of Jesus.

  7. By me, the nakedness of my father’s house shall be taken away, in the name of Jesus.

  8. The foundation that gives my enemies strength, die, in the name of Jesus.

  9. That which killeth my neighbor shall not kill me, in the name of Jesus.

  10. Every wilderness that wants to eat me shall scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Whether my situation likes it or not, Pharaoh, let me go, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    This year, there shall be a new oil on my head, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    I call forth my promotion from the camp of the enemy, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Darkness shall not rejoice over my life, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Any power hunting for me this year, I cut you off, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Affliction by night, die, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Any power that has dug a hole for me shall fall into it, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Every work of wickedness assigned against me, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every evil hand upon my star, wither, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    I recover ten fold all my wasted years, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    This year, I shall not labour in vain, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Let the wind of the Lord blow away all the powers blocking my breakthroughs, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Darkness shall not take glory on my life, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Robbers that steal by day and affliction by night, die, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    O Lord, this year, I enter into the chariot of Your fire, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Let the whirlwind of the Lord burn the heads of witchcraft gathering that is against my life, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Let every wickedness targeted at my life arise and harvest itself, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    I step out from the company of my equals, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Every sorrow afflicting my possessions, be consumed to ashes, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Avengers of the past, be silenced, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Every heart of stone lifted against me be shattered, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Every enchantment assigned for my downfall, die, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    Satanic visitors in my dreams, that have wasted my possessions, die, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    Every eater of good things in my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    Sudden destruction will not be my lot this year, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    My portion shall not be given to another, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    Every king Uzziah sitting on the throne of God in my life, die, in the name of Jesus.
