Praise and Worship

Scripture Reading: Exodus 20

Confession: Isaiah 43:19

PRAYER POINTS

  1. Every foundational curse in my life, break, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Every foundational covenant in my life, break, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every foundational bondage in my life, break, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Thou power of deliverance, fall upon me now, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every foundational arrester, be arrested, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every darkness planted in my foundation, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Every Goliath and Pharaoh witchcraft, die, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Every serpent in my foundation, die, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Every scorpion in my foundation, die, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    O axe of God, work in my foundation, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Let God arise and let all foundational witchcraft scatter, in Jesus name.

  12.    Every seed of witchcraft in my foundation, die, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Every foundational confusion, die, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Blood of Jesus purge my foundation.

  15.    Holy Spirit shake down every foundational stronghold, in Jesus name.

  16.    Every foundational familiar spirit, I bind you and I cast you out.

  17.    Every foundational marine power, bow, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Every spirit that entered me through curse, go now, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every seed of poverty in my foundation, die, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Every foundational padlock, break, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Every problem attached to my family name, be neutralized, in Jesus name.

  22.    Every problem that stands as Pharaoh, my rod shall swallow you today.

  23.    Legal right of satanic harassment, break, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Every power searching the oracle to know my progress, die in Jesus name.

  25.    Family idols, receive the consuming fire of God, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Every dream poison, come out of my life, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    According to the mystery of Elisha, as I smite the ground, the enemy shall bow.

  28.    I release myself from any bondage present in my family line, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Any evil pattern laid by my ancestors, break, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    No enchantment shall hold me captive, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    I break the pattern of darkness that locks me up, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    I release my life from the yoke of my village, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    I shall walk and live in liberty, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    Every curse that came to me through the sins of my ancestors, break, in Jesus name.

  35.    Every ancestral transmission of affliction, break and die, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    Lord, I thank you for answers to my prayers.
