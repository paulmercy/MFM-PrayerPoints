When God visits a nation, a family or an individual, it could either result in good or bad for the enemy. It could result in a disaster, while peace, joy and happiness are for the children of God. The Lord visited the land of Egypt because of the children of Israel. He did mighty unforgettable wonders. When a person call on God day and night without giving Him a breathing space and is expectant, the visitation of God might be earlier than expected or He could decide not to come as early as they think. But one thing is certain, He will surely come. When God comes, one’s spiritual life and physical life will change. He wipes clean all filthiness for Him to answer by fire. When the Lord visits you, your life will experience a turnaround breakthrough, good things will spring forth, everlasting joy will be your lot; you will laugh last and experience deliverance from all bondages. Your fears will be handicapped and the enemies will fear you. You will have victory over your enemies, bad dreams will come to an end, sicknesses will disappear; bitterness, long-time sorrow, misfortunes, bad luck, marital challenges and affliction of all forms shall disappear. I decree into your life that the God that created Dr.Daniel Olukoya, who gave him the vision of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, sustaining the vision from dying and established this ministry will establish good things in your life in the name of Jesus. You are the next to testify, congratulations.

  1. Oh Lord I thank you for all you have done for me.

  4. Oh Lord, wash me with the blood of Jesus from all the stains of sin.

  15.    By the visitation of the Almighty God, let every gadget used by the enemy to monitor my testimony, be set ablaze, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Let the foundation of my life be shaken by the divine visitation of God, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Every necromancer from my family line, going to the grave yard for evil purposes against my life and marriage, die in the name of Jesus.

  18.    O God my father, appear and scatter every witchcraft meeting in my father’s house to desolation, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    By the visitation of the Almighty God, you spirit husband/wife, be roasted and die, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    God of Elijah, visit every unfriendly friend and disgrace them in the name of Jesus.

  21.    You the mystery of witchcraft powers of my father and mother’s house, be revealed, scatter and die, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Midnight enemies that have made it their point of duty to substitute me for horses to their witchcraft meetings that is causing miscarriage, die in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Evil strongmen that must not see the sun in order to destroy my destiny, host of heaven destroy their habitation and kill them, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Witchcraft dream thieves that have stolen my menses, by the anger of God, return it by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Midnight traders that have turned my body into their warehouse, public announcement, power has changed hands, I evacuate your goods and burn them to ashes by the fire of the Holy Ghost, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Every hand of the wicked against the plan of God for my life, be roasted in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Anything in my body rendering my womb weak to carry my children, I shake you off now, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Every hole in my womb causing miscarriage, blood of Jesus cover it up now, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Oh Lord, let my enemies hear the thunder of your voice and let them be confused and perish, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    O God that visited Obed-Edom and changed his life, visit me in the name of Jesus.

  33.    The Lord that heard the prayer of Abraham, Isaac, and Zachariah, visit me and let my sperm produce glorious children, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    Oh Lord, by your power that visited the Virgin Mary and brought about the Saviour of the world, do the hard thing in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    The corpse of Lazarus heard your voice in the tomb, every good thing that is dead in my life, by the power of the living God, resurrect by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    By the covenant and death of Jesus on the cross of Calvary that brought about redemption, I claim redemption today in every area of my life, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    Jesus Christ had laid His life for me, I shall not miscarry, I shall not have still birth, and I shall not lose any good thing again, in the name of Jesus.
