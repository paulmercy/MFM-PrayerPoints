CONFESSION: Numbers 20:8, Psalm 91; Matthew 24:1-2

Praise Worship

Confess these words with faith in your heart (3 times). "Oh Lord, my God, I thank you for loving me. I know you have good things in stock for me. Lord Jesus, I thank you or your steadfast love and faithfulness to me and my family. My Father and my God, as I open my mouth to pray this warfare prayers Oh Lord, answer me by fire, in the name of Jesus."

  1. I receive power to overcome every spirit of the rock, in the name of Jesus.

  2. You spirit of rock, release my destiny by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  3. You power of the rock, release my blessing by the Blood of Jesus.

  4. All my blessings deposited inside the rock, receive fire and locate me now, in the name of Jesus.

  5. You power of the rock, assigned to afflict me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every rock of poverty, break and release my money by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Anything programmed against me inside the rock, break to pieces, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Rock of Ages, fight against every rock of failure in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Every evil load in my life, come out by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   Anything planted in my life by the enemy, die, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   You evil stone in my body, come out by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   Every stone of problem in my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   Oh Lord, purge me with your fire, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   Every evil material in my body, die, by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   Every evil monitoring power inside the rock assigned against my life, break by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   Begin to thank the Lord for answered prayers.
