Scripture Reading - Colosians 2

Confession - Col. 2:14-15
  1. I rise above every foundation dark covenant by the power in the blood of Jesus.

  2. I pull the stronghold of covenant judgement battling against me down, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every dark covenant covering my glory, break, in the name of Jesus.

  4. My destiny, break away from demoting covenants, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Witchcraft covenants assigned to frustrate my life, break, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every covenant empowering my enemies, die, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Covenant of short life, break, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Mystery of covenant violation troubling my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Covenants with tragedy and sorrow, break, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    I pull down the stronghold of biting covenants, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Any covenant behind my problems, die, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Covenant with spiritual powerlessness, break, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Every strongman attached to any covenant troubling my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    I revoke every collective dark covenant, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    My blood, reject destructive covenants, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Let Your healing hand be stretched out upon my life now, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Let Your miracle hand be stretched out upon my life now, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Let Your deliverance hand be stretched out upon my life now, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Lord, terminate the life of all sicknesses in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    I dismantle every engagement with the spirit of death, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    I rebuke every refuge of sickness, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    I destroy the grip and operation of sickness, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Every knee of infirmity, bow, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Let my negativity be converted to positive, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    I command death upon all sicknesses, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    I shall see sicknesses no more, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Let the whirlwind scatter every vessel of infirmity, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Every spirit hindering my healing, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Let all death contractors kill themselves, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Let every germ of infirmity die, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Let every agent of sicknesses die, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Hidden sicknesses, disappear, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    Fountain of discomfort, dry up, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    Every dead organ, receive life, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    Let my blood be transfused with the blood of Jesus.

  36.    Every internal disorder, receive order, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    Every infirmity, come out with all your roots, in the name of Jesus.

  38.    I withdraw every conscious and unconscious cooperation with sicknesses, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  40.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in Jesus’ name.

  41.    O God arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries.

  42.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
