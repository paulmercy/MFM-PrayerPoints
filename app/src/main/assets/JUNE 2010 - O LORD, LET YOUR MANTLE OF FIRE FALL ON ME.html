Praise worship

Scripture Reading - Acts 2

Confession - Luke 4:18
  1. Every wicked grip upon my life, loose your hold, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Holy Ghost fire, destroy every garment of reproach, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Let every stubborn enemy experience God as a mighty terrible one, in the name of Jesus.

  4. O Lord, bring miracles into my life by ways which my enemies cannot find, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Just as the grave cannot hold Jesus, no grave will hold my miracle, in the name of Jesus.

  6. I receive fire to quench every satanic opposition, in the name of Jesus.

  7. My prosperity shall not depend on the tongues of men, in the name of Jesus.

  8. O Lord, give me the fire that kills death, in the name of Jesus.

  9. O Lord, purge my life by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Holy Ghost fire, burn away all that is not holy in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    O Lord, let Your fire generate power in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Every programme of failure fashioned against my destiny, die, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Every plan of witchcraft against me, fall into pieces, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Every evil bird swallowing my money, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Every arrow of poverty, go back to your sender, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Every serpent and scorpion of poverty, die, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    I refuse to be the footmat of poverty, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    I cast the spirit of the snail out of my finances, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    I bind every evil word spoken against my prosperity, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    O Lord, rearrange my situation to favour me, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    O Lord, rearrange my situation to glorify Your name, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    O Lord, rearrange my situation to defeat and disgrace my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    O Lord, speak life to my dry bones, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    O Lord, speak deliverance to any bondage situation in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    O Lord, speak clarity unto every cloudy situation in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    I determine the destiny of my environment by the blood of Jesus.

  28.    I speak death unto every infirmity, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Every arrow of affliction, go back the way you came, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    I recapture every lost opportunity by the blood of Jesus.

  31.    I command my foreign benefits to locate me, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Evil birds, receive the arrow of fire, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    Witchcraft opposition, receive the rain of affliction, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    My prosperity shall not become history while I am still living, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  36.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  38.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
