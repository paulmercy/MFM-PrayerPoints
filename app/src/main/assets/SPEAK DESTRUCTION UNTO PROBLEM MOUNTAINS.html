Praise worship

Scripture Reading - 1 Kings 18

Confession: Job 22:28 says,“Thou shalt also decree a thing, and it shall be established unto thee: and the light shall shine upon thy ways.”

PRAYER POINTS:

  1. Every strange power gathered against my advancement be destroyed in the name of Jesus.

  2. Lord, send Your angel to sow terror and panic into the hearts of all evil doers gathered for my sake in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every satanic vigils against me scatter in the name of Jesus.

  4. Let all the eaters of flesh and drinkers of blood stumble and fall in the name of Jesus.

  5. O Lord change my present speed and grant me new speed according to Your will in the name of Jesus.

  6. Ancestral wall built around my glory be pulled down in the name of Jesus.

  7. O Lord divide the tongues of the enemy in the name of Jesus.

  8. My glory that is under bondage receive deliverance by fire in the name of Jesus.

  9. Mountains before me I dash you to pieces in the name of Jesus.

  10.    My breakthroughs in captivity come forth in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Every unprofitable tree growing in any department of my life be uprooted in the name of Jesus.

  12.    My potentials that are caged by the enemies receive deliverance in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Every foundational curse be uprooted in the name of Jesus.

  14.    My growth that is under restriction be released in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Every evil tree that the father has not planted in my life be uprooted in the name of Jesus.

  16.    You evil tree be cast into the fire in the name of Jesus.

  17.    All the chains of multiple curses in my life be broken down in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Blood of Jesus begin to wash away all multiple curses working against my life in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every good thing that has been paralyzed as a result of multiple curses receive the power of resurrection now in the name of Jesus.

  20.    No power of multiple curses shall have any hold upon my life, I receive total freedom now in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Today Lord I am loosed and freed from the power of multiple curses in the name of Jesus.
