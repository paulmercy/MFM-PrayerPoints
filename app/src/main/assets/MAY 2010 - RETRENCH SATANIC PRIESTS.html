Praise worship

Scripture Reading - 1 Kings 18

Confession - Isaiah 44:25

  1. Every coven finger pointed against me, wither, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Power of incantations, uttered to demote me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every assignment of dark hunters for my star, wither, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Jeroboam forces of my father’s house, die by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  5. O Lord, cover me with Your precious blood, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Cleanse me, O Lord, in the living waters that flow from Your heart, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Eyes of darkness assigned to monitor me, wither, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I retrench every satanic priest ministering against by any evil altar, in the name of Jesus.

  9. O Lord, pass through the land in violent anger and slay the wicked, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Any power making covenants with bad spirits to destroy me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Unrepentant witchcraft, die the death of the uncircumcised after the order of Goliath, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    O God, arise and cut off the horns of the wicked, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    O God, arise and overthrow the wicked, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    O Lord, reveal any aspect of my life that is not pleasing, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Anyway that could give satan a foothold in my life, be wiped out, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    I trample down on the wicked counsellors, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    I break and dissolve all traps and diversions, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    I sever the transmission of all satanic vows, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    I prophesy death on wicked witchcraft, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    O God, arise and hang my Hamaan, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Dark altar diviners, loose your powers, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Any ladder of the enemy in my life, favouring satanic priests, I cut you off, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    By the powers that render the diviners mad, I pursue mu pursuers, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    O Lord, I place myself at the foot of Your cross, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    O Lord, surround me with Your holy light, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    O Lord, let Your healing waters purify my family line of satan and sin, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    I reclaim any territory that was handed over to satan, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    All emissaries of satanic headquarters, I bind your power, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Any evil spirit that has attached itself to me in any way, be cut off, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    I proclaim Jesus Christ as Lord of all circumstances and difficulties in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    I bind and reject all familiar spirits and spirits of affliction, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    I bind and I reject all spirits of division and disruption, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    I bind and reject all interlocking spirits, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    I come against all satanic vows, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  36.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  38.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
