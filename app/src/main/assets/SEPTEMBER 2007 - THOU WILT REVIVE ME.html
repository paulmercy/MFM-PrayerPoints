Praise worship

Scripture Reading - Ezekiel 37

Confession - Psalm 138:7
  1. My Father, whether I wake or sleep, let Your presence go with me, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Oh Lord, deliver me from the natural darkness of my mind, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Oh Lord, deliver me from the daily snares that are assigned against me, in the name of Jesus.

  4. I declare that I shall not grieve or resist the Holy Spirit, in the name of Jesus.

  5. My Father, increase my lot for You, in the name of Jesus.

  6. My Father, help me to listen to Your voice every day, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Oh Lord, let me study and stand for discipline, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Oh God, come as power, expel every rebellious lot from my heart, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Oh God, come as teacher, and fill me with all understanding, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Oh God, come as light, and illuminate the Scriptures unto me, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Oh God, let me see my sins as the nails that bind You to the cross, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Oh Lord, let me see my sins as the swords that pierce You by Your side, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Oh God, give me a deeper knowledge of Thy self, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Oh God, give me deeper power in private prayers, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Oh God, dig me deep and fill me to overflowing with living waters, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    My Father, purge me from every false desire and every unprofitable aspiration, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Oh God, arise, kill my envy and command my tongue, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Oh God, arise and deliver me from attachments to unclean things, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    My Father, open to me the spring of divine knowledge, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Oh God, while I live, let my life be exemplary, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    My Father, let me never slumber nor sleep the sleep of spiritual death, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Oh God, take me, sanctify me and use every faculty that I have, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    My Father, focus my mind on You and turn my trials to blessings, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Oh God, give me deadness to the world, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    My Father, let every part of my character and conduct make a serious divine impression on others, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Every power assigned to injure the prosperity of my soul, die, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Every spirit of slumber, I bury you today, in Jesus’ name.

  28.    Every spiritual cataract, clear away from my vision, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Every witchcraft power drinking the blood of my spiritual life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Strength of God, empower me, in Jesus’ name.

  31.    I refuse to tarry in the valley of powerlessness, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Anything planted within me that has not manifested now but will manifest in future to make me backslide, dry up, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  34.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in Jesus’ name.

  35.    O God arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries.

  36.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
