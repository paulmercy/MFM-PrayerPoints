PRAISE WORSHIP

CONFESSION: Col. 2:14-15

SCRIPTURE READING: Psalm 106
  1. I take authority over this year, in the name of Jesus. I decree that all the elements of this year will cooperate with me.I decree that these elemental forces should refuse to cooperate with my enemies. I speak unto the sun, the moon and the stars. They must not smite me. I pull down every negative energy planning to operate against my life this year. This is the year the Lord has made, I will rejoice and be glad in it. I dismantle any power uttering incantations to capture this year. I render such incantations and satanic prayer null and void. I retrieve this year out of their hands, in the name of Jesus. Spirits of favor, counsel, might and power, come upon me, in the name of Jesus. I shall excel this year and nothing shall defile me. I shall posses the gates of my enemies.

  2. The Lord shall anoint me with the oil of gladness above my fellows. The fire of the enemy shall not burn me. My ears shall hear good news and I shall not hear the voice of the enemy. My future is secured in Christ, in the name of Jesus.God has created me to do some definite services. He has committed into my hands some assignments which He has not committed to anybody. He has not created me for nothing. I shall do good. I shall do His work. I shall be an agent of peace. I will trust Him in whatever I do and wherever I am. I can never be thrown away or downgraded. There will be no poverty of body, soul and spirit in my life this year. The anointing of God upon my life gives me favor in the eyes of God and man all the days of my life. I shall not labor in vain. I shall walk this year in victory and liberty of the spirit.

  3. Covenant and power of kolanut and alligator pepper linking me to evil ancestral powers, die, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Covenant and power of any libation linking me to evil ancestral powers, die, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Power of any mark or incision linking me with evil ancestral powers, die, in the name of Jesus.

  6. I separate myself from any evil tree representing my destiny, I curse the tree to die, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Thou power of evil handshake, die, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Thou power of evil laying of hands on my head, die, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Every naming ceremony ritual linking me with evil ancestral powers, die, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   Evil powder or pomade linking me with evil ancestral powers, die, in Jesus' name.

  11.   Every pronouncement upon my life in favor of evil ancestral powers, die, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   Any conscious or unconscious evil initiation from the womb, break and scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   Let the power and consequence of any witchdoctor treatment upon my life die, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   Any traditional ceremony linking me up with evil ancestral powers, die, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   I reject and vomit every evil communion taken with evil ancestral spirits through evil festivals, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   Any food taken from the table of the devil, die, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   Guarding evil ancestral angels, loose your hold upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   Any sacrifice exposing me to the evil ancestral powers, die, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   I break and terminate the power and influence of family idols over my life, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   Ancient and recently invited and uninvited idols in my family line, I terminate your powers, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   Every evil water inside me bearing witness and linking me to evil ancestral powers, I vomit you now by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   Any evil water baptism holding me in bondage to evil ancestral powers, die, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   Any wrong use or disposal of my placenta, linking me with evil ancestral powers, I cancel you now, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   Any evil promise or debt linking me with evil ancestral powers, I settle it with the blood of Jesus.

  25.   Any cowry revealing my secrets, die, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   Any bead, bangle or rings linking me with evil ancestral powers die, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   Any gift implicating me before evil ancestral powers, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   Any sand linking me with evil ancestral powers, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   Any power hiding under evil ancestral cover to fight me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   I reject the government of evil ancestral powers upon my family line, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   I cut off every spiritual umbilical cord linking me with evil ancestral powers, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   I blot out my name and record from every evil ancestral register, in Jesus' name.

  33.   Every evil ancestral pollution, release my life, by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   I recover any lost glory, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   Victory of evil ancestral powers over my life, I bury you today, in Jesus' name.

  36.   Every arrow of sickness from evil ancestral powers, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   Every evil ancestral mortar pounding my blessing, die, in the name of Jesus.

  38.   Any evil ancestral pot swallowing my blessings, vomit them by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  39.   Every evil trade by batter, concerning me, made with evil ancestral powers, break, in the name of Jesus.

  40.   Any money or coin linking me to evil ancestral powers, die, in the name of Jesus.
