Praise worship

Scripture Reading - Luke 13:10-17

Confession - Psalm 138:8

PRAYER POINTS

  1. My glory, receive resurrection, in the name of Jesus.

  2. I arrest every power arresting my breakthroughs, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Yokes of satanic delay on my breakthroughs, break, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Fire of God, arise, bombard the camp of my oppressors, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every power behind my problems, die, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Fire of God, arise in anger and destroy every destroyer targeted at my life, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Every unseen fire, burning in my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Every gathering of slaughterers against my life, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Dream acquired yokes in my life, break, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    My life, disobey the voice of enchanters, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Every citadel of frustration, targeted against my life, break, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Let multiple chaos pursue my pursuer, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Every conspiracy assigned to demote me, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Holy Ghost fire, burn every deposit of darkness in my body to ashes, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Every evil hand, pointed against my star, wither now, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    My Father, arise by fire, and defend my portions, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Let the belly of the earth release my buried virtues, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Every commandment of darkness, against my destiny, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Any satanic intermediary, assigned against me, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Let locations of arrows assigned against me catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Let the powers of the emptiers surrounding my breakthroughs die, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Any power assigned to demote my destiny, die, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Let the magnet of infirmity backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Thou power of God, break every yoke of limitation upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Wealth of the gentiles, arise and locate me, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Powers of affliction and oppression, targeted against my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    O finger of God, arise, command my lifting up, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Every power assigned against my foreign wealth, die, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Let the favour, that brings repossession, locate my life, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Every well that I have lost, open, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Well of prosperity, open unto me, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    My stolen property, trouble the thief, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    Gates of death, reject my life, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    Every appointment with death, be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    Every garment, exposing me to attack, roast, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    Every strange thing in my flesh, come out and die, in the name of Jesus.
