Praise worship

Scripture Reading Genesis 41

Confession Psalm 71:21
  1. O Lord, align me to Your divine agenda, in the name of Jesus.

  2. O Lord, plug me into Your divine calendar, in the name of Jesus.

  3. My Father, let me walk in Your timing, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Let my days co-operate with the plan and purposes of God, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Let the will of satanic agents for my life be dismantled, in the name of Jesus.

  6. I claim heavenly diplomatic immunity over my life, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Every evil targeted against my dwelling, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Evil arrow shot against me by day, die, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Night terror assigned against me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Every power assigned to frustrate me, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Every good thing that is wrongly aligned in my life, receive divine alignment, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    My Father, anoint me to think creatively, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    My Father, bring me into new realms of power, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    My Father, bring me into new dimension of divine inspiration, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    My Father, upgrade my brain and mind by the power in the blood of Jesus.

  16.    Cycles of death and failure, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    I silence every wind assigned to bring problems into my life, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    My Father, assign Your angels as my security escorts and supernatural security, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Let the angels of God arise and repossess my occupied territories for me, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Gate of death, close, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Doors of affliction and confusion, close, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    New effectual doors of opportunity, open unto me by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Multiple rivers of income and cash flow, locate my life, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    My Father, make me sensitive to the wave length of the Holy Spirit, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Let every good thing I touch turn to prophetic gold, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    O Lord, grant me wisdom to do my work more effectively, professionally and accurately, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    I call forth by fire, all individuals and resources divinely assigned to move me forward, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Anything or anyone assigned to demote and frustrate me, move away, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    I receive power to excel in productivity, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   Let me always maximize my potentials, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Power of greatness, come upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Power of inspiration, come upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    Power of uniqueness, come upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    Power of problem-solving ideas, come upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    Power of vision, come upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    Power of wealth, come upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    Power of intelligence, come upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  38.    Power of success, come upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    Power of wisdom, come upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  40.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  41.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in Jesus’ name.

  42.    O God arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries.

  43.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
