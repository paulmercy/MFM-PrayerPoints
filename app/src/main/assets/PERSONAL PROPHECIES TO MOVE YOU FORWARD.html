Praise worship

Confessions - Personalize these prophecies by confessing each prophecy seven times.

PRAYER POINTS
  1. I shall not quench the fire of the Holy Spirit.

  2. I am filled with all spiritual blessings in the heavenly places.

  3. I have resurrection power within me.

  4. My words are increasing in power and force.

  5. I possess a merry heart that doeth good like medicine.

  6. God has given me dominion.

  7. I shall cooperate with God in naming the kind of life I want to life.

  8. I am complete in the Lord Jesus Christ.

  9. I speak and think of whatever is of good report.

  10.   My God shall supply all my needs according to His riches in glory.

  11.   The Lord is my helper, I will not be afraid what can man do to me.

  12.   The name of the Lord is a strong tower, I run into it and I am safe.

  13.   I will not speak what comes to my mind but will speak what is in God’s mind.

  14.   My faith comes by hearing as I listen to the Word of God.

  15.   The hand of the Lord is upon me. As I prophecy great things.

  16.   My inward man is renewed day by day by the power of God.

  17.   My youth is renewed like the eagle. My eyes are not growing dim nor is my strength gone.

  18.   I hear the sound of an abundance of the rain of blessings coming my way.

  19.   I am a king, I reign in life through Jesus Christ.

  20.   I live by faith and not by sight.

  21.   I tread upon all serpents and scorpions, they cannot harm me.

  22.   I am divinely insured, no weapon fashioned against me shall prosper.

  23.   I make Jesus the great physician my doctor.

  24.   I forbid satan to put any disease in my body.

  25.   The joy of the Lord is my strength.

  26.   God’s word is life and health unto all my flesh.

  27.   Every part of my body functions perfectly.

  28.   I use the power of God to meet any need.

  29.   I do not lack any good thing.

  30.   I am blessed so that I can be a blessing.

  31.   I refuse to be poor, Christ has redeemed me from the curse of poverty.

  32.   Poverty and lack are underneath my feet.

  33.   I am the head and not the tail.

  34.   Wealth and riches are in my house.

  35.   My faith can come under tests, but I will pass the tests.

  36.   I will not become bitter towards anyone, I will pray for those who slander me.

  37.   I am an overcomer because I live by faith and not by sight.

  38.   God has not given me the spirit of fear, but of power, love and a sound mind.

  39.   I have mountain-moving faith, I speak to mountains and they obey me.

  40.   I do not fear the future because I trust in God.

  41.   I am the property of the Lord Jesus Christ.

  42.   I dwell in the secret place of the most high.

  43.   The power of God is in me and no foe can withstand me.

  44.   The angels of the Lord encamp me and deliver me from every evil work.

  45.   I am redeemed from the curse of sickness and I refuse to accept its symptoms.

  46.   No evil shall befall me, neither shall any plaque or calamity come near my dwelling.

  47.   The word of God is medication and life to my flesh.

  48.   I put on the whole armour of God and the shield of faith protects me from the fiery darts of the wicked.

  49.   God keeps all my bones and not one of them is broken.

  50.   God redeems my soul from the power of the grave.

  51.   I have life and in my pathway there is no death.

  52.   I am released from the bondage of the fear of death.

  53.   I cast my cares upon the Lord because He cares for me.

  54.   I have the glorious divine inheritance working inside of me.

  55.   I reject every tradition that does not conform to the Word of God.

  56.   I shall not utter words that would disrespect God’s authority.

  57.   I shall breakthrough not break down.

  58.   The world shall read of my rising and not of my falling.

  59.   I shall not roam aimlessly in the market square of life.

  60.   I will not drink the water of affliction.

  61.   My David, arise and kill your Goliath.

  62.   I make God’s word my words.

  63.   I will walk in love and by faith in God’s word.

  64.   All things are possible with God and I choose to believe in Him.

  65.   I release Christ in me, the hope of glory.

  66.   I have the keys of the kingdom of God, whatever I bind is bound in heaven, whatever I loose is loosed in heaven.

  67.   My success is not dependent on luck or chance, it has to do with Jesus.

  68.   I shall not die but live to declare the glory of God.

  69.   I am following God’s plan for my life.

  70.   I shall not been hindered by disappointments and discouragements.
