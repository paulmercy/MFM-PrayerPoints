Scripture Reading: Mark 10

Confession: 2 Cor. 10:4

AGGRESSIVE PRAISE WORSHIP
  1. O God arise, and uproot anything You did not plant, inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus name.

  2. O God, let the fire of revival, fall upon the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O God, let the power of peace and progress, overshadow this nation, in the name of Jesus.

  4. O God, arise and give us God-fearing leaders, in Jesus name.

  5. Blessings that swallow poverty, come into my life, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every dark stronghold, holding my blessings, I render you powerless, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Plans and expectations of oppression for my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Every satanic contractor, hired against me, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Every strange fire, prepared against me, catch your owner, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   I speak destruction unto every tongue speaking against me, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   My Father, arise and disgrace the battle disgracing me, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   My Father, let me know, let me see and let me experience Your power, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   Wherever men have ridiculed me, I will be celebrated, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   O God, arise and convert my insult to glory, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   My honour, stolen by the enemy, be returned seven fold, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   The eye to see my glory, O God, arise and give it to me, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   Any power that has sold me, O God, arise and cage them, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   Arrow head of the battles of my life, be exposed and disgraced, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   O God that troubled the enemy of the Israelites, arise and trouble my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   Powers, planning my nakedness, die by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   Any power that wants me to be disgraced, die by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   Any curse assigned to waste my life, break by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   Doors of mistakes, opened for my life, close by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   Every curse of rejection and neglect, break, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   O God, arise and mock the mockers of my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   Any power that wants me to see my mates and hide for shame, die, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   Powers, assigned to make me naked before my helpers, I bury you now, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   Any power assigned to turn me to a laughing stock, die, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   Thou power of envious witchcraft, die, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   Dreams of failure, threatening my breakthroughs, die, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   O God of times and season, break the pattern of sorrow in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   Agreement of darkness to snatch my favour, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   Blood of Jesus, visit my yesterday and repair it for your glory, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   Altar of nakedness, pursuing my life, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   Favour that has never happened in my family line, manifest in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  36.   I wash my head with the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   Strange serpents, assigned to stop me, I stop you before you stop me, in the name of Jesus.

  38.   Witchcraft animals, sent against me, receive blindness, in the name of Jesus.

  39.   Powers, using evil birds to slow down my destiny, die, in the name of Jesus.

  40.   My sleeping blessings, resurrect by the power in the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.
