Scripture Reading: Exodus 14

Confession: Isa. 43:19

AGGRESSIVE PRAISE WORSHIP
  1. O God arise, and uproot anything You did not plant, inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  2. O God, let the fire of revival, fall upon the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O God, let the power of peace and progress, overshadow this nation, in the name of Jesus.

  4. O God, arise and give us God-fearing leaders, in Jesus’ name.

  5. Storms of disappointment and frustration, come out of my life, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every curse of failure in my life, receive the hands of success, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Evil powder, blowing into the air to send my blessings away, expire, in the name of Jesus.

  8. O God, arise and cancel the agenda of the enemy to waste my life, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Powers, making evil pronouncement and issuing evil decree on my family, become useless, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Any strong curse, following my life around, to make my life miserable, blood of Jesus, break them now, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Eagle of my life, in the pit of darkness, receive freedom and jump out by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Strange birds, connecting me to strange battles, blood of Jesus, terminate them now, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Powers, speaking death on my destiny, be wasted, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Every destroyer of joy, in my family, enough is enough, leave us alone and die, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Every celebration that is not of God, that is against my life, be scattered by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Storms of wicked elders, assigned to swallow me in the day of my joy, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Any power, assigned to use me as a test of demonic powers, run mad and die, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Powers, wasting my opportunities, die by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every yearly failure and yearly disappointment, assigned to make me useless, die, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Powers, torturing me because of my glory, Lion of Judah, tear them up, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    My name, hear the word of the Lord, depart from every evil altar, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Dark powers, discussing my destiny for destruction, hear the word of the Lord, receive the fire of the God of Elijah, in Jesus’ name.

  23.    Powers, hiding my name from greatness, die, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Powers, saying until I serve other gods, I will continue to suffer, O God, arise and disappoint them, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Battles, blocking up my destiny, scatter by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Wicked powers, waiting for me to be destroyed before they leave me, lick the dust and die, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Powers, protecting my battles from dying, your end has come, die, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Stubborn problems, caused by blood in my life, receive destruction, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Agents of darkness that have vowed that I will accept defeat as my fate, die, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Fire of God, destroy the gates that are making my enemies to boast against me, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Fire of God, consume the gate that is giving the enemy ladders into my life, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Lord, let the memory of the wicked over my life expire, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    O ground, cry against my battles and swallow them, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    O God, arise and disgrace my blackmailers, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    Lord, let the struggles of the wicked enemies against me be in vain, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    Father, let the curse of the Lord swallow the head of my battle, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    My enemies shall fall by the sword they’ve prepared for me after the order of David, in the name of Jesus.

  38.    Strange powers gathered to fight me, destroy yourselves, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    Battles that open door for troubles for me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  40.    Powers, assigned to use my body as habitation of sickness, I set you ablaze, in the name of Jesus.
