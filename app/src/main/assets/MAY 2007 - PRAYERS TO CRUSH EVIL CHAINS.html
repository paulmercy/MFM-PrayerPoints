Praise worship

Scripture Reading - 1 Chronicles 20

Confession -Psalm 31:1
  1. Rivers of iniquity, coming down from my generation, I stop you before you stop me, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Any evil flow passing down to my children, I break your power, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every evil associated with my family line, I cut it off by the blood of Jesus.

  4. Iniquity shall not be found in my camp, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Any impurity in my blood or my body, I stop you before you stop me, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every evil seed sown into my life and those of my children, die, in the name of Jesus.

  7. I and my family, shall increase in wisdom and be in favour with God and man, in the name of Jesus.

  8. My family shall not listen to the voice of the strangers, in the name of Jesus.

  9. I shall not wither, spiritually, mentally or physically, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Foolishness shall not be bound up in my heart, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    I shall not be a companion of fools, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    I shall be far from oppression and tormentors shall not come into my camp, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    O God, arise and hide me from the strife of tongues, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    I shall not defile myself with the world, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    The sun shall not smite me the day nor the moon by the night, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    The rod of the wicked shall not rest upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    My household and I shall eat the best of the earth, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    My body is the temple of God, sickness shall not dwell in it, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every disguise enemy in my life, be uncovered, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Every evil gang up against my life, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    My head shall never lack oil, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    The blood of the Lamb shall always speak for me at the gates, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Every power assigned to add sorrow to my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    I shall not be a tool in the hands of the enemy, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Anger and bitterness shall not be found in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    I am shielded from the curses of men by the blood of Jesus.

  27.    The sound of rejoicing shall not depart from my house, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    I shall not fall into the snares of my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    The law of the Lord shall not depart from my mouth, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    As my days, so shall my strength and security be, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Blood of Jesus, speak for my household and myself, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  33.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in Jesus’ name.

  34.    O God arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries.

  35.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
