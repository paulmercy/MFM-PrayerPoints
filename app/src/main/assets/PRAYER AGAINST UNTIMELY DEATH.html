Praise and worship

Oh God my Father, I come before you to make the following confession of your unfailing words because you said confession brings possession. By my words you will justify me. Let the words of my mouth and the meditation of my heart be acceptable in thy sight. None of my words shall fall to the ground. Let my prayer be your delight and let the wishes of my enemies be an abomination unto you. With conviction of heart, with boldness in my spirit, with certainty in my expectation. With the power in my tongue and with the assurance you have always given unto me that whatsoever i bind in the world shall be bound in Heaven and whatsoever I loose in this world shall be loose in Heaven. Therefore l decree and declare to all the host of my adversaries, dark powers, principalities household wickedness, environmental wickedness, office hold wickedness and even Church hold wickedness that l am destined to have good health like Caleb; diseases of my environment shall not touch me, long life is my portion, l Shall see my children’s children, my health shall not fail me, l shall not be a candidate of Hospital bed, assassins, ritual killers, kidnappers. Armed robbers shall not locate me or any member of my family; evil-Doctors report on my health shall not hold, dream of death anybody had about me or any member of my family. l cancel you and you will not come to pass by the blood of Jesus. I shall not die but live to fulfill my destiny in the mighty name of Jesus. (14xAmen)
