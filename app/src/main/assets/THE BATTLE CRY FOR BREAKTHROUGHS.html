Praise worship

Scripture Reading - Psalm 91

PRAYER POINTS:
  1. I take authority over this year, in the name of Jesus. I decree that all the elements of this year will cooperate with me. I decree that these elemental forces should refuse to cooperate with my enemies. I speak unto the sun, the moon and the stars. They must not smite me. I pull down every negative energy planning to operate against my life this year. This is the year the Lord has made, I will rejoice and be glad in it. I dismantle any power uttering incantations to capture this year. I render such incantations and satanic prayer null and void. I retrieve this year out of their hands, in the name of Jesus. Spirits of favor, counsel, might and power, come upon me, in Jesus name. I shall excel this year and nothing shall defile me. I shall posses the gates of my enemies.

  2. The Lord shall anoint me with the oil of gladness above my fellows. The fire of the enemy shall not burn me. My ears shall hear good news and I shall not hear the voice of the enemy. My future is secured in Christ, in the name of Jesus. God has created me to do some definite services. He has committed into my hands some assignments that He has not committed to anybody. He has not created me for nothing. I shall do good. I shall do His work. I shall be an agent of peace. I will trust Him in whatever I do and wherever I am. I can never be thrown away or downgraded. There will be no poverty of body, soul and spirit in my life this year. The anointing of God upon my life gives me favor in the eyes of God and man all the days of my life. I shall not labor in vain. I shall walk this day in victory and liberty of the spirit.

  3. O Lord, keep me from leaning and relying on my own understanding and intelligence.

  4. O Lord, pull down imaginations and every high thing in my life that are not of God.

  5. O Lord, purify my lips with Your holy fire.

  6. O Lord, let my fellowship with You become greater.

  7. O Lord, enable me to become the person You created me to be.

  8. I bind all evil spirits in me or that is attacking me, in the name of Jesus.

  9. I receive the mouth and the wisdom which my adversaries are not able to resist, in Jesus’ name.

  10.    I stand against every satanic operation that would hinder my prayer this year, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Satan, I refuse your involvement in my prayer life this year, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    I claim the victory of the Cross for my life this year, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Every satanic foothold in my life, be dismantled by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    I tear down the stronghold of satan against my life, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    I smash every plan of satan formed against me this year, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    I confound every stubborn pursuer this year, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    I bind every power cursing my destiny into ineffectiveness, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    I strike with chaos and confusion every evil power that will siphon my blessing this year, in Jesus name.

  19.    I turn the evil devices of household witchcraft upside down, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    I render every local satanic weapon against my life this year harmless, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    I receive deliverance from the spirit of anxiety, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    I grab every stubborn problem and smash it against the Rock of my salvation, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    I reverse every satanic calendar for my life this year, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    O God arise and uproot any thing You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries.

  27.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
