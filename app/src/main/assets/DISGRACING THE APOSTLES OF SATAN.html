Praise worship

Scripture Reading - 1 Kings 18

Confession - Isaiah 7:7-11

PRAYER POINTS

  1. O God, arise and visit all shrines assigned against me with thunder and earthquake, in the name of Jesus.

  2. All satanic altars, erected against my destiny, be overthrown by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O God, arise and set unquenchable fire upon every coven of darkness assigned against me, in the name of Jesus.

  4. All images, carved against my life, break into pieces after the order of Dagon, in the name of Jesus.

  5. O God, arise and cause confusion in the camp of my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  6. I decree that my oppressors shall consult their powers in their confusion, but there will be no response, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Holy Ghost fire, arise and stir up civil war in the camp of my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I command every satanic intermediary working against me to loose heart, in the name of Jesus.

  9. O God, arise and bring the plans of my oppressors to nothing, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    My Father, set up a fierce king against my oppressors and let the king treat them with great torture, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Let the power of God intimidate and frustrate all diviners assigned against me, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    O God, arise and wipe out the understanding of my stubborn pursuers, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    O God, pour out the spirit of dizziness upon all evil spiritual consultants speaking against me, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Let these evil spiritual consultants stagger in all they do as a drunkard staggers in his vomit, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    By the power in the blood of Jesus, let all soothsayers, witchdoctors, enchanters arrange against me be cut off, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    O heavens, O earth, hear the word of the Lord: you must not execute the counsel of my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    O God, pass through the camp of my enemies with affliction and drain the anointing of wickedness, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Every power assigned to wreck my destiny, your end has come, die, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    I de-program and cancel all negative prophecies pronounced against me, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    O Lord, guide me into the mysteries of my life.

  21.    O Lord, give me the keys to unlock the hidden riches of secret places.

  22.    All ancient doors that have hindered the plan of God for my life, be unlocked by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    I destroy every agreement made at covens and satanic centers against me, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Every secret code, evil registers and archives of the enemy in my place of birth, be roasted, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    O God, arise and cast abominable filth upon witches and wizards and set them as gazing stock, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Let the tables of witches and wizards become snares unto them, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Let the eyes of witches monitoring my life be darkened, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Let the covens of witchcraft become desolate, let there be no one to dwell in them, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    I command crash-landing of witches and wizards assigned against my breakthrough, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    I command the sun to smite my oppressors in the day and the moon and stars to smite them at night, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    I command the stars in their courses to fight against my stubborn pursuers, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    O God, arise, roar and prevail over my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    Let the gathering of the wicked against me be harvested for judgment, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    O God, arise and hang every Haman assigned against my life, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    Every stumbling block to God's prophetic agenda on my life be rooted out, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    All negative words that have been spoken against me by evil men die, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    All evil records, evil marriage certificates and registers that are kept in satanic archives against me, be wiped off by the blood of Jesus.

  38.    I program divine health, divine favour, long life, spiritual advancement into my life by the power in the blood of Jesus.

  39.    I close down every spiritual ship carting away my benefits, in the name of Jesus.

  40.    O God, arise and lay waste all the operations of dark forces working against me, in the name of Jesus.
