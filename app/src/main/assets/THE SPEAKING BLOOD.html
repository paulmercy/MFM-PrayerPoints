Praise and worship

Scripture reading- Exodus 12, Hebrew 12

Confession Rev. 12:11

PRAYER POINTS

  1. In the name of Jesus, I plead the blood of Jesus.

  2. In the name of Jesus I apply the blood of Jesus over my house.

  3. In the name of Jesus, I soak myself and family in the blood of Jesus.

  4. In the name of Jesus, I apply the blood of Jesus Demons, you cannot enter my house.

  5. I drew the bloodline of protection around my property.

  6. Let the blood of Jesus speak confusion into the camp of the enemy.

  7. Let the blood of Jesus speak destruction unto every evil thing in my life, home and family.

  8. Let the blood of Jesus; speak peace unto every broken marriage.

  9. I sprinkle the blood of Jesus on all my properties.

  10.   You evil power, I bring to bear the power of the blood of Jesus to bind you.

  11.   I render every power militating against me impotent by the blood of Jesus.

  12.   Minister death, unto the enemy of progress in my life and family by the blood of Jesus.

  13.   I bind the staying power of any problem in my life and family by the blood of Jesus.

  14.   I speak boundary against you devil by the blood of Jesus.

  15.   I end my family enter the holy of holies by the blood of Jesus.

  16.   I hold blood of Jesus, against spirit of starvation in any area of my life and family.

  17.   I hold the blood of Jesus against demonic delay of my miracles.

  18.   I hold the blood of Jesus against failure at the edge of success.

  19.   I hold the blood of Jesus against lack of good helpers.

  20.   I hold the blood of Jesus against fruitless efforts in my life and family.

  21.   I hold the blood of Jesus against occupying wrong positions.

  22.   I hold the blood of Jesus against every delayed and denied promotions.

  23.   I hold the blood of Jesus against dead accounts.

  24.   I hold the blood of Jesus against evil diversion.

  25.   I hold the blood of Jesus against lost foreign benefits.

  26.   I hold the blood of Jesus against vagabond anointing.

  27.   I hold the blood of Jesus against profit starvation.

  28.   I hold the blood of Jesus against tortoise and snail anointing.
