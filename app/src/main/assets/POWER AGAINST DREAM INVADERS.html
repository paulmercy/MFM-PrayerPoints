PRAISE AND WORSHIP
PRAYER POINTS
  1. Every witchcraft Spirit assigned to trouble my sleep fall down and die, in Jesus name.

  2. Every satanic power violating my dream state be destroyed by the thunder of God in Jesus name.

  3. Let the fire of God consume and destroy every masquerade troubling my dream in Jesus name.

  4. Let the sword of God’s fire cut off the head of every satanic animal troubling my dream in Jesus name.

  5. Holy Spirit unmark every human or demonic agent hiding behind any mask to trouble my sleep in Jesus name.

  6. Every evil mark in my spirit, soul and body attracting dream invaders be erased with the blood of Jesus.

  7. Let my sleep and dream state become too hot for any strange spirit to violate in Jesus name.

  8. Let the military angels of God be on perpetual guard duly top protect my sleep and dreams in Jesus’ name.

  9. I nullify with the blood of every evil effect of satanic dreams on any area of my life in Jesus’ name.

  10.   Let any man or woman projecting into my dreams to oppress or violate me be destroyed by the thunder of God in Jesus name.

  11.   Let the ground open up and swallow any evil altar where my name is being mentioned for evil in Jesus’ name.

  12.   Let the thunder of God answer whenever my name is mentioned for evil in Jesus name.

  13.   I withdraw the control of my body, soul and spirit from any satanic manipulation in Jesus name.

  14.   Any object representing me for evil on any evil altar physical or spiritually disintegrate in Jesus name.

  15.   Let a might terrible noise overcome any power or person seeking to oppress me in my dreams in Jesus’ name.

  16.   Let the spirit of confusion overwhelm anyone planning to invade my dream in Jesus name.

  17.   Every satanic animal assigned to trouble me in my dream go back and destroy your sender in Jesus name.

  18.   Every demonic memory storing information about me be afflicted with the spirit insanity in Jesus name.

  19.   Every power working in my dream be afflicted with the spirit of insanity in Jesus name.

  20.   I receive the anointing to remember clearly all of my dreams in Jesus name.

  21.   I receive the grace and ability to effectively participate in my dreams in Jesus name.

  22.   Let my dreams come under the total control of the Holy Spirit in Jesus name.

  23.   Father Lord inspire me to dream in line with your will for my life in Jesus name.
