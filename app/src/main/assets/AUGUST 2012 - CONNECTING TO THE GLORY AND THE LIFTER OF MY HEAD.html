Scripture Reading - Psalm 3

Confession - Psalm 3:3

Praise worship
  1. Thou power of artificial glory, die by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Powers biting my glory, loose your hold and die, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Power to move from glory to glory, come upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  4. I kill every glory killer by the power in the blood of Jesus.

  5. Voices from the grave yard calling my glory, shut up, in the name of Jesus.

  6. My Father, my Father, my Father, cover my defenceless head, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Arrows fired at my glory, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Every power dancing to cut off my head, die, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Every contest against my internal Moses, expire, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    My foundational glory, where are you, appear, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Every witchcraft siren, assigned to confuse my star, die, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Anything buried or planted anywhere to swallow my glory, die, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Lifter of my head, Jesus Christ, arise and lift me up, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Satanic command assigned to make my star wander, die, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Belly of glory swallowers, burst, release my glory, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Glory aborters, hear the word of the Lord, my life is not your candidate, die, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Every power mandated to bury my glory, your time is up, die, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Every power assigned against my day of glory, die, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Arrows from the grave, fired against my glory, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Glory swallowers, vomit my glory, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Every power troubling my glory, the King of glory shall trouble you today, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Glory of my ancestors buried by idolatry, come forth, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Glory of my family line, swallowed by iniquity, I recover you, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    My family glory, hijacked by dark powers, be released now, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    O King of glory, arise, change me from glory to glory, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Every Goliath, defying my glory, die, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Powers assigned against my glory, die, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Every power of Ichabod upon my destiny, die, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Every problem tormenting my glory, die, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    My legs, reject backwardness, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    The glory of my life stolen in the womb, I recover you by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Glory manipulators and glory robbers, let me go, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    Yoke magnetizers, hear the word of the Lord, die, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    My days shall not be restless but shall be called the day of the Lord, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    Any good thing that has died in my life when I was ignorant, come alive, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    My eyes, open to see what God is doing, in the name o f Jesus.

  37.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  38.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  40.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
