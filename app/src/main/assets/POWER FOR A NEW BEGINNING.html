Praise and worship

Confession: Isaiah 43:19:

"Behold, I will do a new thing; now it shall spring forth; shall ye not know it? I will even make a way in the wilderness, and rivers in the desert."

PRAYER POINTS

  1. Thank the Lord for enabling you to see this day.

  2. I close every evil door open against my life in the past, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O God of new beginning, arise and manifest Your power in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  4. My Father, arise and open new chapters of great breakthroughs in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every demotion and stagnation assigned against my destiny, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  6. O God, arise and do what will make the world to know that You are my God, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Every demonic foundation troubling my destiny, clear away, in the name of Jesus.

  8. O God, arise and reveal any hidden talent in my life to me, in the name of Jesus.

  9. My Father, show me Yourself and show me Your ways, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Any power fuelling my problems, dry up, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    O God, arise and mock every gathering of my adversaries, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    I break every witchcraft coven with a rod of iron, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Every plan against my glory, be scattered, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Every priest of darkness monitoring my destiny, dry up, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Evil power house assigned against me, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Demonic grave diggers assigned against me fall into your own graves, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    O God, arise, distract my enemies with problems that are bigger than them, in Jesus’ name.

  18.    Miracle, that surpasses explanation, manifest in my life now, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    O God, arise today, and let my situation change, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Today, I position myself by fire for divine intervention, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    O God, my Father, burst forth in my life by signs and wonders, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Resources of heaven, arise by fire, promote me, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    My Father, arise and let the root of hardship in my life die now, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    I recover ten-fold all my wasted years, in the name of Jesus.
