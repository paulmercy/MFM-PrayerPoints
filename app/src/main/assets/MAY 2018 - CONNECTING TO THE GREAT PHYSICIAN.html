Scripture Reading: John 5

Confession: Isaiah 53:5

AGGRESSIVE PRAISE WORSHIP
  1. O God arise, and uproot anything You did not plant, inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

    2.  
  2. O God, let the fire of revival, fall upon the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O God, let the power of peace and progress, overshadow this nation, in the name of Jesus.

  4. O God, arise and give us God-fearing leaders, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Father, let the Holy Spirit fill me afresh, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Father, let every unbroken area in my life be broken by the touch of fire, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Holy Ghost fire, incubate my life, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Every anti-power bondage, in my life, break by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  9. All evil strangers, flee from my spirit and let the Holy Spirit take control, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   O Lord, catapult my spiritual life to the mountain top, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   Father, let heavens open and let the glory of God fall upon me now, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   Father, let signs and wonders be my lot, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   Father Lord, perform a creative miracle in every area of my body requiring such, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   Every altar of affliction of sickness, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   Every water of affliction of sickness flowing into my life, be cut off, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   Any evil association against my health, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   O God, arise and make me whole, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   My body, resist and reject every killer disease, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   O God, arise and let every enemy of my sound health scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   Healing anointing, come upon my home, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   If there is anything in me that is not of God, I don’t want it, Depart now, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   O earth, vomit anything that has been buried inside of you against my health, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   Health arresters, be arrested by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   Eaters of flesh and drinkers of blood, assigned to my life, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   I break the backbone and destroy the root of every spirit speaking against me, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   Parasites, viruses and bacteria of infirmity, my body is not your habitation; die, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   My body, be redeemed from the power of sickness and diseases, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   My immune system, become unchallengeable by any infirmity, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   My flesh, my blood, reject the voice of death, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   Power drinking the blood of my wholeness, die, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   I charge my body with the fire of the Holy Ghost, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   O Great Physician, deliver me now, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   Anything I have eaten or swallowed, presently working against my health, be dissolved by the power of God, in Jesus’ name.

  34.   I receive fresh energy, fresh fire, fresh power into every organ of my body, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   Every dark prophecy, dark prayer, dark wish against my well-being, I dash you to pieces, in the name of Jesus.

  36.   Rain of divine immunity, envelope and soak me, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   Blood of Jesus, laminate my life, in the name of Jesus.

  38.   Lord Jesus, transfuse me with Your blood.

  39.   O Lord, heal me and I shall be healed, in the name of Jesus.

  40.   My Father, make me a mysterious wonder, in the name of Jesus.
