Praise worship

Scripture Reading - 2 Kings 7

Confession - Malachi 3:1
  1. O God of 24 hour miracle, where are You? Appear in my situations now, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Where is the Lord God of Elijah, divide my Jordan by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Voices speaking against my rising up, shut up, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Pharaoh of my father’s house, let me go, in the name of Jesus.

  5. O God of the suddenly, arise in the thunder of Your power, appear in my situations, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Jehovah, the story changer, arise, let my story change, in the name of Jesus.

  7. O God of 24-hour miracles arise, locate me by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  8. By the power in the blood of Jesus, O God arise and disappoint my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Satanic barriers to my breakthroughs, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   I shall not see shame, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   I shall not see demotion, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   I shall not see defeat, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   Uncommon and extraordinary testimony, locate me by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   My Father, do what will make me to forget past years of pain, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   Miracles that will wipe out my past ridicule, manifest, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   Every power behind my stagnation, die, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   Every evil power using my glory to shine, die, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   My Father, arise and let my way open, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   My Father, empower my angels of blessings to breakthroughs now, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   My past labours, arise, become testimonies, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   I move from glory to glory by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   I move from strength to strength by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   I move from grace to grace by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   I move from favour to favour by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   Power speaking demotion into my destiny, die, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   Where I have been failing, I will succeed, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   Every good thing I touch shall prosper, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   If I’m carrying sand now, it shall become gold, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   If I’m carrying dust now, it shall become silver, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   If I’m carrying mud now, it shall become diamond, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   Let the unction to function successfully come upon me now, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  33.   Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  35.   Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
