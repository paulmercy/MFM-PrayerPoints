Scripture Reading - Psalm 35

Confession - Isa 60:1
  1. I separate myself from any evil tree representing my destiny, I curse the tree to die, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Sentences of the enemy upon my destiny, die, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Let the elements gather their resources to favour and promote my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  4. I establish the power of God upon you, o earth, bless my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  5. You elements, you shall not hurt that which the Lord has blessed in my life, you shall not steal from my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Thou power of evil handshakes, die, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Thou power of evil laying of hands, die, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I use the keys of heaven to lock out problems from my life, in the name of Jesus.

  9. That which the devil has locked against me, open by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Anyone using dust and leaves against me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Woe unto the powers bragging and boasting against my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Kidnapping power, I rebuke you by thunder, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Every robber of destiny, assigned against my life, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    O night, arise, gather strength against my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Every intimidation of the wicked, break, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    I wipe out the relevance of my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    My Father, let Your angels be provoked against my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    By fire, by thunder, I war in the spirit of Elijah and Jehu, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every gathering of the wicked against my destiny, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Every power rising up against my last laugh, die, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    I pass the decree, o earth, call forth those that should bless me and those that should bring money to me, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Mantle of divine acceleration, fall upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Thou power of bad luck, die, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Thou power of set back, die, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Any power flying to arrest my progress, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Power of reproach, die, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Any problem programmed to ridicule me, expire, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Agents of the grave summoned to appear in my dreams, die, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Vision killers, I stop you before you stop me, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Anything burned to bury me, bury your owners, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    O heavens, arise in thy might of perfection, perfect all that concerns me, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    O Lord, repair what should be repaired in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    O God, arise, and disgrace every enemy of my increase, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    O God, arise, and speak words of creation into my situations, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    O God, arise, and cancel every consequence of any mistake made by me, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    O God, arise, and let every enemy of my calling scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  38.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  40.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
