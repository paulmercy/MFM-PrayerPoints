Scripture Reading: Mark 10:46-52 

Confession: Matthew 11:12

  1. Circular problems, assigned to my life, you will not prosper, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Every satanic project, against my breakthrough, explode in the face of the enemy, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every dream of backwardness, go back to your senders, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Any power, working round the clock, with dark powers, against my life, perish, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every household Cain, assigned to waste my Abel, you will not succeed, rush to your grave and die, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every domestic enemy, anointed by satan, to terminate my life, terminate your own life, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Anti-Christ power of my father’s house, assigned to punish me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Every satanic contact of my father’s house, hunting for my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Every giant, occupying my promised land, loose your hold, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    By the power that silenced Sennacherib, I silence my adversaries for ever, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Every wicked altar, harbouring my name and my picture, collide with thunder and die, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Every affliction, targeted at me, explode in the hands of your owners, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Every king Saul of my household, pursuing my David, die, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Satanic grave digger of my father’s house, dig your own grave and enter into it, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Any power that has joined witchcraft and occult group to attack me, thus saith the Lord, suffer not a witch to live, lose your life for my sake, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Arrows of shame, disgrace and mockery, fired into my life, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Arrows of rise and fall, fired at me, expire, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Every vulture of darkness, assigned to eat my flesh, go back to your senders, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Every verdict of darkness, issued against me, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Every dominant wicked power of my father’s house, I bury you now, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Every satanic traditional manipulation, assigned to remove my glory, fail, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Any power, assigned to make me irrelevant in my generation, your time is up, die, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Any power, giving me a deadline to die, fall down and die on your own deadline, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Every strange material and strange deposit, in my body, disappear now and go back to your senders, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Every satanic payroll, where my enemies registered my name, I delete my name and substitute it with the names of the enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Any wicked hand, collecting evil against me, decay and die, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    By the power that silenced Haman in favour of Mordecai, O Lord, let every power assigned against my existence, die, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Any power assigned to manipulate my destiny, enough is enough, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Every assembly of the wicked, delegated to destroy my destiny, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    Every ancient strongman, labouring to waste my efforts, my life is not your victim, expire, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    Every wicked mouth, sowing evil seeds against me, I command the seeds to catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    Every ancient gate, standing against my breakthroughs, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    I plug my destiny, into the mystery of divine favour, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  38.    Every enemy, of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  40.    You fire of revival, fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
