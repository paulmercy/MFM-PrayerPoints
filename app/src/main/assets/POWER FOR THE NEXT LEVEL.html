Scripture Reading - Acts 2

Praise and worship

Confession - Ps 75:6
  1. Every Red Sea harassing my life, give way, in the name of Jesus.

  2. O God, arise complete what You have started in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  3. My life, hear the word of the Lord, disappoint my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  4. I shall bury the enemy assigned to terminate my life, in the name of Jesus.

  5. I bind and cast out every power of discouragement and fear, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every yoke affecting my destiny, break, in the name of Jesus.

  7. My Father, show me Your hidden riches, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I declare that I am untouchable and unstoppable, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Wickedness of the wicked, expire by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    O Lord, draw out Your sword and fight for me, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    My unrepentant enemy, hear the word of the Lord, receive reproach, insult and injury, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    O God, reveal to me the secret plans of my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Every evil factory, manufacturing against me, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    O Lord, change me and use me to change my world, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Men shall begin to help me without knowing me, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Where I was tolerated, I shall be celebrated, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Where I was rejected, I will be selected, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    The power that promoted Joseph, shall change my story, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    My breakthrough is not negotiateable, I move forward by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    I cancel every satanic agenda against my expectation, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    My Father, accelerate my celebration, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Every power prolonging my problem, die, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    My star shall be honored in the land of the living, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    My Father, make me Your workshop and Your showroom, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    O God, arise and perform miracles beyond explanation in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    After the order of Daniel, O God, raise helpers for me, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Holy Ghost, announce my destiny by fire and thunder, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Yoke multipliers, your time is up, die, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Weapon of witchcraft targeted against me, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    The mouth that attacks me shall be silenced, in the name of Jesus.
