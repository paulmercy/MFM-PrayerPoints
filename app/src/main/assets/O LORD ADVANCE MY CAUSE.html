Praise worship

Bible Reading: Deut 6

Confession: Is 41:15, 16; 62:1-3; Jer 51:20-23: Jn 18:37

PRAYER POINTS

  1. Thank God for his power and love that is unchangeable.

  2. Confess your sins consciously and unconsciously committed unto God.

  3. Repent from all sins and cover them with the blood of Jesus.

  4. Every satanic decree against my promotion, excellence and progress be nullified in the name of Jesus.

  5. You satanic prison, release me in the name of Jesus.

  6. O God, arise, let every witchcraft power and their activities against me scatter altogether in Jesus name.

  7. I refuse the household enemy to swallow me in Jesus name.

  8. Every wall of Jericho hindering me from entering my promise land, receive the hammer of God and scatter completely in the name of Jesus.

  9. O Lord, let your unchangeable (unfailing) power establish my promotion in Jesus name.

  10.   You evil followers, fashioned against mc, stumble, fall and die in Jesus name.

  11.   Let every spiritual padlock and evil chain hindering my spiritual growth, be roasted in the name of Jesus.

  12.   Every spiritual power above me planning to suffocate my vision and destiny, die in Jesus name.

  13.   Let the craftiness, wisdom and devices of the ancient made to destroy my vision and destiny, fall by fire in Jesus name.

  14.   O Lord, let there be a change in my life and in my situation by tire in Jesus name.

  15.   Let the power of God to mount up as eagle, fall upon my life in Jesus name.

  16.   Every witch planning to build wall of Jericho around my destiny, fall down and die in Jesus name.

  17.   I pull down every strange throne erected against me in Jesus name.

  18.   Every foothold of the enemy upon my destiny, be destroyed in Jesus name.

  19.   O Lord, let your favour upon my life place me above my contemporaries, in Jesus name.

  20.   O God the terrible one, possess me with your power in Jesus name.

  21.   O Lord, sharpen me by your tire and use me for your glory, in Jesus name.

  22.   The sun of my progress will set in Jesus name.
