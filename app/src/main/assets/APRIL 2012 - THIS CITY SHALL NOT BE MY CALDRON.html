Scripture Reading - Ezekiel 11

Confession - Ezekiel 11:11

Praise worship
  1. I decree that this city shall not be my caldron, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Every caldron chain around my legs, be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Wherever my feet shall touch, divine dominion shall take over, in the name of Jesus.

  4. I break off every poison from my life, in the name of Jesus.

  5. My tongue shall not serve satanic purposes, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Let my tongue release a two-edged sword to destroy the camp of my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Everything that is mine in the earth, be released, in the name of Jesus.

  8. As from this day, no caldron shall prosper against me, in the name of Jesus.

  9. You caldron,I break your authority from the roots, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   Everywhere I enter, the caldron there shall fail, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   I release my children, property, business from the caldron, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   Let the host of heaven destroy every caldron assigned against my life, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   I close every satanic chapter on my life, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   O Lord, let me be the continuation of Your kingdom, whether men encourage me or not, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   By judgement, I pursue my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   I shall not be called a slave any more, but I shall be called the salvation of the Lord, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   Caldrons assigned against my destiny, be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   Every caldron summoning me to the grave, be silenced, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   O caldron, stumble and break, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   I magnetize back all my lost blessings, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   Every curse of the wicked fashioned against me, wither, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   O God, repair every aspect of my life, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   You son of the prince of the air, I cast you out of my life, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   Every environmental witchcraft assigned to trouble my star, scatter unto desolation, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   Every power assigned to trouble my life, destroy yourself, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   I separate evil from my pattern, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   I cast evil out of my sweat, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   Anything that weakens the power in my tongue, I cast you out, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   Anything boasting against me in the flesh, I cast you out, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   I destroy every bondage in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   I uproot their wickedness, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   I rebuke every environmental caldron assigned against me, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   O God, overhaul my existence by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   Let my lips and my mouth be powerful, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   Every power assigned to use me as a sacrifice this year, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  36.   No evil thing shall stand against my life, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  38.   Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  39.   O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  40.   Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
