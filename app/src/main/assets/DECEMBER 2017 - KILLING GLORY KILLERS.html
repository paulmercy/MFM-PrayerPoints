Scripture Reading: Psalm 3

Confession: Psalm 84:10

Aggressive Praise Worship
  1. The powers that diverted my parents to failure, you will not succeed over my life, in the name of Jesus.

  2. The failure of my parents will not be repeated in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Satan will not be able to divert my life to useless activities, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Blood of Jesus, redeem me, in the name of Jesus.

  5. I bind and cast out every power causing internal warfare, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every demonic doorkeeper locking out good things for me, be paralysed by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  7. I command every evil power fighting against me to fight against and destroy each other, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Every breakthrough hindering, delaying, preventing, destroying and breaking demons, receive confusion, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Divine power and control, attack the spirits of violence and torture, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   O Lord, let there be a civil war in the kingdom of darkness, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   Every evil monitor of my life, be destroyed by the fire of God, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   Thou power of God, penetrate my spirit, soul and body, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   Association of demons gathered against my progress, be roasted by the thunder fire of God, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   I disobey any satanic order programmed by inheritance unto my life, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   Every satanic decision taken against my progress, be nullified, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   Every evil deposit in my spirit, soul and body, be flushed out by the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   O Lord, my God, promote me in the spiritual and in the physical, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   Every stranger in my body (ministry, life and calling), jump out, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   Any satanic arrow fired against me, go back, locate and destroy your sender, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   Holy Ghost, arise and destroy the habitation and works of the wicked in my life (home, finances, ministry), in Jesus’ name.

  21.   Every serpentine spirit spitting on my breakthrough, be roasted, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   Every enemy of the perfect will of God for my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   I bulldoze my way into breakthroughs, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   I revoke every satanic decree upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   I revoke every satanic decree upon my family, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   I revoke every evil decree upon my prosperity, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   I revoke the satanic decree of profitless hard work, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   Thou power of impossibility in my destiny, die, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   Bad dreams, go back to your senders, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   You the strongman in charge of my family, I render you impotent, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   Blessings, honour, prosperity, and favour, locate me, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   I become a bone in the throat of the enemy, I cannot be swallowed, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   I revoke every evil law controlling my life, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   All the witches and wizards in charge of my case, you will know no peace until you release me, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   O God, give me the power to be focussed in the name of Jesus.

  36.   Every plan to divert my life through sin, fail, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   O thou, that troubleth the Israel of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today, inthe name of Jesus.

  38.   Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  39.   O God, arise and uproot, anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  40.   Fire of revival, fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
