Praise worship
Scripture Reading - Acts 19

Confession - Obadiah 1:3-4

PRAYER POINTS:
  1. I repent from all ancestral idol worship, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Every idol of my father’s house, lose your hold over my life, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every strongman of the idol of my father’s house, die, in the name of Jesus.

  4. I silence the evil cry of any idol fashioned against me, in the name of Jesus.

  5. All consequences of ancestral idol worship upon my life, I wipe you off by the blood of Jesus.

  6. Holy Ghost fire, burn down all spiritual shrines of my father’s house, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Oppression agenda of my family idol, die, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Every blood speaking against my generational line, be silenced by the blood of Jesus.

  9. Every idol power speaking against my destiny, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   I break all ancestral covenants with any idol power, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   Every bitter water flowing in my family from any idol, dry up, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   Any rope tying my family line to any family idol break, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   Every landlord spirit troubling my destiny, be paralyzed, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   Every outflow of satanic family name, die, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   I recover every benefit stolen by idol powers , in the name of Jesus.

  16.   Where is the God of Elijah, arise, disgrace every family idol, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   Every satanic priest ministering in my family line, be retrenched, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   Arrows of affliction originating from idolatry, loose your hold, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   Every influence of idol worship in my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   Every network of idol power in my place of birth, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   Every satanic dedication that speaks against me, be dismantled by the power in the blood of Jesus.

  22.   I vomit every food with idolatrous influence that I have eaten, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   Every unconscious evil internal altar, be roasted, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   Let the stone of hindrance constructed by family idol be rolled away, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   The voice of foundational idols will never speak again, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   Every strongman assigned by the idols of my father’s house against my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   Every satanic promissory note issued on my behalf by my ancestors, be reversed, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   Garments of opposition designed by ancestral idols, roast, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   Every satanic cloud upon my life, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   My glory buried by family idols, come alive by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   Thou power of strange gods legislating against my destiny, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   Idols of my place of birth, I break your chain, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   Thou vagabond power assigned against me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   I puncture the imagination of oppression targeted against me, in the name of Jesus.
