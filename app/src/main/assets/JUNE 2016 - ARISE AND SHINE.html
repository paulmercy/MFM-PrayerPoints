Scripture Reading: 1 Samuel 17

Confession: Isaiah 60:1-2

Aggressive Praise Worship
  1. I refuse to be a victim of failure, in the name of Jesus.

  2. I refuse to be a victim of limitation, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O Lord, let all my long time delayed blessings come forth by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  4. After the order of King David, I pursue all my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  5. After the order of King David, I overtake all my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  6. After the order of King David, I recover all my stolen blessings, in the name of Jesus.

  7. O Lord, let the blood of Jesus and the wall of fire surround my recovered blessings, in the name of Jesus.

  8. My Father, let every area through which the enemy is stealing from me be blocked by the blood of Jesus.

  9. You angels of God, arrest every thief of blessings assigned against my life, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    I shall not misuse my recovered blessings, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    My Father, carry out a divine surgery that will move my life forward, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Any power cooking my spirit man in a caldron, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Every internal yoke, assigned against me, break, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Satanic summon of my spirit man, in a crystal ball or a dark mirror, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Every evil hand, fashioned to manipulate my life, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Every evil load in any part of my body, go back to your senders, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Where is the Lord God of Elijah, arise and kill every poison in my body, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Every hidden infirmity and silent sickness, die, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every rage of the enemy assigned to send me backward, break, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    By the power of the living God, my portion shall not be transferred to another, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Every strongman of obscurity in my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Every handwriting of ordinances of bad luck assigned against my life, be wiped off by the blood of Jesus.

  23.    Every tree of bad luck planted against my life, be uprooted by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Every ancestral root of failure in my family line, be uprooted by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Every strongman of bad luck and failure assigned against my life, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Every root of bad luck and failure in my life, dry up, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    I reject every inherited bad luck and failure, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Every power keeping me away from my divine helpers, die, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Every power blocking my opportunities, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    You season of failure, frustration and famine, your time is up in my life today, die, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    My life, hear the word of the Lord, refuse to follow the agenda of the enemy, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Every chain and shackle binding me with sin, break by the power in the blood of Jesus.

  33.    I receive the anointing to bless and be blessed, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    Stubborn yokes assigned to my life, fall away, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    O God, arise and let good things pursue and overtake my life, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    By the mercy of the living God, I shall not live my life in sorrow and hardship, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  38.    Every enemy, of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  40.    You fire of revival, fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
