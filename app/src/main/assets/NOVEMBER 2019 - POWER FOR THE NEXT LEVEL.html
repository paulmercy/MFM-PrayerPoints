Scripture Reading: James 5

Confession: Isa. 60:1

AGGRESSIVE PRAISE WORSHIP
  1. O God arise, and uproot anything You did not plant, inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  2. O God, let the fire of revival, fall upon the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O God, let the power of peace and progress, overshadow this nation, in the name of Jesus.

  4. O God, arise and give us God-fearing leaders, in Jesus’ name.

  5. Any power offering sacrifice of death against me, die in my place, in the name of Jesus.

  6. God of this mountain, arise in Your all-sufficient power, make a way for me by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Any dark power, assigned to monitor my life, be disgraced this month, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Enemies of my next level, I command you to expire, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Every evil kingdom, legislating against me, I tear you up, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Any sacrifice, offered to cage my progress, lose your power, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Any power grinding and mixing things against me, be wasted with your materials, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Lord, put upon me the garment of uncommon favour, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Unrepentant blockers of my joy, die in shame, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Powers, assigned to make me a bad example, die by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    By fire, by force, I shall prosper in every desert land, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    I shall not die the death of my enemies. I shall not die with my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Evil arrows, blocking my rain of blessings, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    The finger of the enemy shall not prevail over my life, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every evil cage that has refused to let me go, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Powers, assigned to break my ladder of greatness, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    O God, arise and use my hands for mighty works, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    O God, arise and give me prophetic dreams, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    O God, arise and give me prophetic eyes, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    The third eye of witchcraft, observing my life, receive blindness, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Thou pot of blockage and hindrance, break, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Thou ancestral gate of affliction, break, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Every satanic fire, burning my progress, quench and backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Dark power, cooking my flesh and my health, burn to ashes, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Powers, assigned to sink my blessings, you are liars, die, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Blessings that swallow debt, fall upon me, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Every stronghold holding my blessings, I render you powerless, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Plans and expectations of oppression against me, perish, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    Every satanic contractor, hired against me, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    Every strange fire, prepared against me, catch your owner, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    I speak destruction unto every tongue speaking against me, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    O God, arise and disgrace the battle disgracing me, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    My Father, let me know, let me see and let me experience Your power this month, in the name of Jesus.

  38.    Wherever men have ridiculed me, I will be celebrated, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O God, arise and convert my insult to glory, in the name of Jesus.

  40.    My honor, stolen by the enemy, be returned seven-fold, in the name of Jesus.
