Praise worship

Scripture Reading - 2Kings 19

Confession -1Samuel 2:10
  1. Every witchcraft budget for my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Every witchcraft horn raised against me, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Terrifying noise from the Lord, arise, pursue my Assyrian, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Arrow-head of Assyrian wickedness, depart from my life, go into destruction, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every power mocking my God, die, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every power challenging my faith, die, in the name of Jesus.

  7. O God, arise, send a rumour that will scatter my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  8. O God, arise and break up every siege moulded against my life, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Rumours from God, pursue my pursuers to death, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Scatter them, and destroy their king, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Household witchcraft, I melt your powers, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Evil powers assigned to terminate my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Every dragon power swallowing my prayers, I command your stomach to burst into flames, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Every satanic mask worn by agents of darkness in my dreams, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Every spiritual grave clothes assigned against me, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    I spray the blood of Jesus on the coven of witchcraft in my environment, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Every charm sent to work against me, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Every witchcraft kitchen preparing evil food for me, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every witchcraft tree caging the labour of my hands, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Let the enemy of my destiny be slain in the house of their gods, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    O rumour from heaven, arise, cause defeat, dispersal and death amongst my oppressors, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Let them flee without a pin of all my wealth they have taken, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Goliath, you will not survive your boasting, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    O God, arise, send them a blast, cause them to hear terrifying rumours, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    O God, arise and cause confusion in the camp of my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    I decree that my oppressors shall consult their powers in their confusion, but there will be no response, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Holy Ghost fire, arise and stir up civil war in the camp of my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    I command every satanic intermediary working against me to loose heart, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    O God, arise and bring the plans of my oppressors to nothing, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    My Father, set up a fierce king against my oppressors and let the king treat them with great torture, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Let the power of God intimidate and frustrate all diviners assigned against me, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    O God, arise and wipe out the understanding of my stubborn pursuers, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  34.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in Jesus’ name.

  35.    O God arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries.

  36.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
