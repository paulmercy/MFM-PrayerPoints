Scripture Reading: Psalm 91

Confession: Psalm 4

AGGRESSIVE PRAISE WORSHIP
  1. O God arise, and uproot anything You did not plant, inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  2. O God, let the fire of revival, fall upon the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O God, let the power of peace and progress, overshadow this nation, in the name of Jesus.

  4. O God, arise and give us God-fearing leaders, in Jesus’ name.

  5. O Lord, my God, hide me in Your secret pavilion, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Father, everywhere I go, let Your holy angels encompass me, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Any conscious or unconscious poison of darkness in my body, come out by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Every arrow of darkness, fired into my life in my dream, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  9. O God, arise, be my defence, be my shield and be my buckler, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   My head shall be exalted above all the enemies around me, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   Father, let Your oil of good health physically, spiritually, emotionally, fall upon me, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   I confess and forsake, any sin in my life, assigned to remove my divine protection, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   I shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty God by the power in the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   Any evil assigned to befall me, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   The sun, shall not smite me by day nor the moon by night by the power in the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   I shall prosper, physically, mentally, emotionally, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   Power to be constantly in good health, fall upon me, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   Every rage of ancestral infirmity and generational liability, be wiped off by the power in the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   I charge my body with the fire of the Holy Ghost, therefore, you strangers, depart, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   I receive power to tread upon the lion and the adder by the power in the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   Thou arrow that flies in the noon day, I am not your victim, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   Thou destruction that wastes in darkness, I am not your victim, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   Thou Great Physician, visit me with Your healing hands, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   O God, arise, save me and I shall be saved, heal me and I shall be healed, for Thou are my praise, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   Anything I have eaten or swallowed, physically or spiritually, that is troubling my body, melt away by the power in the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   Disease and infirmity, hear the word of the Lord, my body is not your victim, depart, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   Blood of Jesus, power of the Holy Spirit, glory of the Lord, laminate my life, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   I break every curse and covenant of infirmity, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   Lord, anywhere I go, let the circle of the angels of God surround me, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   Health wasters, protection removers, I am not your victim, depart from me, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   Every worker of iniquity, assigned to manipulate my health and safety, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   I receive the anointing to escape from every tragedy and woe programmed by the enemy, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   Lord, Your word says, the righteous is deliver from trouble, and the wicked comes in his stead, I claim this promise, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   O God, arise and advertise Your words which says, touch not my anointed and do my prophet no harm in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   Any evil imagination against my personality through my names, catch fire, die, in the name of Jesus.

  36.   Any power, calling my name for evil, be paralysed, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   Any dark record, containing my name, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  38.    Any witchcraft coven, assigned to my name, catch fire, in Jesus’ name.

  39.    Any evil tongue, rising up against me in judgement by using my name, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  40.    Every evil imagination, against my destiny through my name, die, in the name of Jesus.
