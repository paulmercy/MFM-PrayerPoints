SCRIPTURE READING - John 21

CONFESSIONS - Rev.13:10

PRAYER POINTS
  1. I come against destiny killers by the resurrection power of the Lord Jesus Christ.

  2. I enforce and effect God’s original plans and purposes over my life, in the name of Jesus.

  3. I decree that no spiritual and organizational weapon formed against my destiny shall prosper, in Jesus name.

  4. I pull down the stronghold and cast down the imagination working against my breakthroughs, in Jesus name.

  5. Anointing that breaks the yoke, break every yoke over my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  6. I establish barricades against the enemies of my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  7. I overrule every satanic sanction which opposes my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I overrule every satanic injunction which opposes my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  9. I overrule every satanic directive which opposes my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    I overrule every satanic mandate which opposes my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    I overrule every satanic decision and ruling which opposes my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    I dispossess spiritual destiny robbers, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Let the angels of fire handle satanic contentions that are against my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Let terror strike the heart of the enemies of my destiny and cause their hearts to fail, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    My Father, contend with those who contend against my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    My Father, arise with Your weapons of war and overthrow the chariots, horses and riders militating against my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    O Lord, establish my name in the heavens and let strangers submit themselves to me, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    My Father, impose my divine destiny over all satanic contentions, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    I bind every satanic surveillance over my life, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    I dismantle every satanic harassment targeted against my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    My life, open to divine deliverance, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    My genes, receive deliverance from inherited problems, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Every power of my father’s house, acting as a door-keeper to my soul, die, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Blood of Jesus, make it impossible for the enemy to track down my star in the realm of the spirit.

  25.    My Father, empower me to increase in skill and understanding, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    My Father, expose my masquerading enemies by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    All visible and invisible walls, against my destiny, break, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    I execute divine judgment against every demonic activity militating against my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    By fire, by fire, by thunder, I war in the spirit of Elijah and Jehu, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Every evil power delaying the manifestations of my destiny, die, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    I operate according to God’s divine time-table and calendar, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    I decree that God’s agenda is my agenda, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    Every witchcraft prayer, targeted at my destiny, die, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    My Father, cast forth lightening to scatter every enemy of my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    I decree nobility and greatness is my portion, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    My Father, frustrate the operations of witches who withstand the anointing after the order of Moses at the court of Pharaoh.
