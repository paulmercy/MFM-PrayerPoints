Praise worship

Scripture Reading - Luke 5

Confession - Joshua 1:8
  1. Every Red Sea harassing my life, give way, in the name of Jesus.

  2. O God, arise, complete what You have started in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  3. My life, hear the word of the Lord, disappoint my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  4. I shall bury the enemy assigned to terminate my life, in the name of Jesus.

  5. I bind and cast our, every power of discouragement and fear, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every yoke affecting my destiny, break, in the name of Jesus.

  7. My Father, show me Your hidden riches, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I declare that I am untouchable and unstoppable, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Wickedness of the wicked, expire by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    O Lord, draw out Your sword and fight for me, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    My unrepentant enemy, hear the word of the Lord, receive reproach, insult and injury, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    O God, reveal to me the secret plans of my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Every evil factory, manufacturing against me, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    O Lord, change me and use me to change my world, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Men shall beg to help me without knowing me, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Where I was tolerated, I shall be celebrated, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Where I was rejected, I will be selected, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    The power that promoted Joseph, shall change my story, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    The power that drought Pharaoh shall change my story, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    My breakthrough is not negotiable, I move forward by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    I cancel every satanic agenda against my expectation, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    My Father, accelerate my celebration, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Every power prolonging my problems, die, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    My star shall be honoured in the land of the living, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Every abortion of my breakthroughs, be uprooted, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    My Father, make me Your workshop and Your show room, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    O God, arise and perform miracles beyond explanation in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    After the order of Daniel, O God, raise helpers for me, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Holy Ghost, announce my destiny by fire and thunder, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Yoke multipliers, your time is up, die, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Weapons of witchcraft targeted against me, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    The mouth that attacks me shall be silenced, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  34.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
