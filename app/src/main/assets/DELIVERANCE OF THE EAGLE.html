Praise worship

Scripture Reading - Acts 16:25-40

Confessions - Isaiah 40:28-31
Make these confessions loud and clear: I am an eagle of God. My wings shall not be clipped by any evil power, I refuse to be caged by frustration, disappointments, destructive habits, worldliness, carelessness, prayerlessness and dark powers, in the name of Jesus. Whether the enemy likes it or not, the eagle of my destiny shall fly. I overthrow every establishment delegated to cage my destiny. The Spirit of the living God shall come upon me and I shall become a new person.
Bring quality repentance unto the Lord

PRAYER POINTS

  1. O eagle of my destiny, arise by fire and confuse my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Every cage of limitation be broken by the power of God, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every root of failure in my life I sentence you to death, in the name of Jesus.

  4. My hidden glory and virtues, manifest, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every serpent and scorpion anointed against my destiny, dry up and die, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every power writing the obituary of my destiny fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  7. I overrule every witchcraft decision taken against my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I refuse to be a negative example, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Power to outshine and excel, fall upon me, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Every evil cycle, causing repeated problems for me, break, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    My Father, set for me a table of promotion in the presence of my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Thou power of God, tear away every garment of shame assigned against me, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    My Father, put a mark on my forehead to qualify me for signs and wonders, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Every power, limiting my potentials, face the anger of the finger of God, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Every enemy of my divinely appointed time, be scattered, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Ever strongman that must die for my breakthrough to manifest, die, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    O mountains, that boast against me, be shattered by the power of God, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Every fountain of bitterness flowing against my life, dry up and die, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    My star, be invisible in the spirit realm to every dark observer, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    My glory, reject the handwriting of darkness, arise and shine, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Every evil power of my fathers house, designed to cage my destiny, die, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    I command every stronghold of darkness in my body to dry up, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Every agenda of affliction for my destiny, dry up and die, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Every power wasting my divine opportunities, be scattered, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    I disallow the establishment of any curse upon my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    I receive the anointing to overcome every power of failure, in the name of Jesus.
