Scriptures: John 12:24; 2 Cor.6; 1 Cor. 9:16; Deut.28:13; Rom.8:35-39

  1. Thank God for the privilege of your calling, in the name of Jesus.

  2. I declare that I am called of God; no evil power shall cut me down, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O Lord, give me power to be faithful to my calling, in the name of Jesus.

  4. I receive the anointing to remain steady, committed and consistent in my ministerial life, in the name of Jesus.

  5. I shall not deviate into politics, church rivalry or rebellion,in the name of Jesus.

  6. O Lord, give me the wisdom to respect my teachers and seniors who have trained me, in the name of Jesus.

  7. O Lord, give me the heart of a servant so that I can experience your blessings everyday, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I receive power to rise up with wings as eagles, in the name of Jesus.

  9. The enemy will not waste my calling, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   The devil will not swallow my ministerial destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   Power for effective development in my calling;come upon me now, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   I declare against spiritual ignorance, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   I bind and cast out every unteachable spirit, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   I receive the anointing for success in the ministry, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   I shall not be an enemy of integrity, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   I shall not steal God’s money, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   I shall not disgrace the call of God upon my life, in the name of God.

  18.   I shall work in holiness everyday, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   I bind the spirit of sexual immorality, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   I receive the culture of loyalty in my ministry, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   I shall not become an old king that is resistant to advice, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   I shall not live a wasteful and extravagant life, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   I shall not serve my wonderful Saviour for filthy financial gain, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   I bind every spirit of quarrels and opposition from my wife/husband, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   My wife/husband shall not scatter my church members, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   Every Judas in my ministry,fall into your own trap, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   My ministry will not destroy my marriage, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   My marriage will not destroy my ministry, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   My children will not be misfired arrows in my ministry, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   I claim progress and excellence for my ministry, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   My church shall experience prosperity, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   O Lord, let my ministry reach the unreached, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   Multitude will go to heaven because of ministry, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   I kill every attack on my ministry; I shall prevail, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   I shall not bite the fingers that fed me, in the name of Jesus.

  36.   I shall not engage in rebellion, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   Every Power of my father’s house working against my calling, die, in the name of Jesus.

  38.   Anointing for excellence, fall upon me, in the name of Jesus.

  39.   Lord, break me and re-mould me, in the name of Jesus.

  40.   I will not surrender to the enemy, in the name of Jesus.

  41.   I shall not die before my time.

  42.   I shall not capture the prosperity of Naaman, in the name of Jesus.

  43.   Let God arise and let every enemy of my calling scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  44.   I receive fresh fire and fresh anointing, in the name of Jesus.

  45.   Holy fire of revival, fall upon my branch, in the name of Jesus.
