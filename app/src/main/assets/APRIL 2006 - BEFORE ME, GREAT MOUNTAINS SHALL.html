Praise worship
Scripture Reading - Acts 12,
Confession - Zech. 4:6-7

Prayer Points

  1. O Lord, I put myself in Your hands.

  2. I promise to do what You want me to do, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O Lord, I will make the decision You want me to make.

  4. Lord, like a potter in the hands of the clay, make what You want out of my life, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Take me, O Lord, and give me whatever shape You want.

  6. If You want, O Lord, break the clay of my life to pieces and start over again.

  7. Lord, do with me whatever You want.

  8. Let it be done according to Your will in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Let it be done according to Your word in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   Let divine patience settle in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   Every power asking for my head in a charger, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   By the power that killed Haman in place of Mordecai, let those who hate my existence be disgraced, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   Agents of sorrow that are pursuing me, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   Terminators of my father’s house, die, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   Satanic sacrifices made for my sake, be rendered impotent, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   Circle of confusion, break, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   Thou city of confusion in my life, be destroyed, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   My hands are blessed, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   My feet are blessed, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   I soak the root of all sicknesses in my life with the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   Wherever my spirit has been locked up, break loose by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   I will not fail as salt of the earth, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   Fire of God, roast all witchcraft bags holding my finances, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   Evil blood-sucking powers, suck yourselves, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   I dismantle every blockage in the dream, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   Every satanic bank manager, die, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   I terminated the appointment of the spirit of backwardness, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   Spirit of spiritual slumber, get out of my life, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   Every wall of Jericho erected against my success, be scattered, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   Pharaoh of backwardness, I bury you today, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   Every unrighteous swearing of my ancestors, be cancelled, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   Every dark hand in my foundation, fighting my destiny, wither, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   Anything stolen from my destiny in the womb, I recover you by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   Any power waiting for my day of honour to disgrace me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   Every power promoting captivity in my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  36.   Lord, visit me with unusual surprises, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  38.   Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in Jesus’ name.

  39.   O God arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries.

  40.   Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
