Scripture Reading: 1 Kings 19

  1. Let the law behind stubborn walls be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   Let my words carry divine fire, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   Let the armies of heaven wage war against my oppressors, in the name of Jesus.

  36.   O earth, vomit every wickedness against me, in the name of Jesus.
