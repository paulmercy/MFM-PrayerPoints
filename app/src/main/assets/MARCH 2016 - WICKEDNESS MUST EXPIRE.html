Scripture Reading: Esther 7

Confession: Psalm 10:15

Aggressive Praise Worship
  1. My Father, my Father, my Father, cause a positive radical change in my situation and destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Every poison of the enemy in my blood, I neutralize you with the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.

  3. My Father, my Father, my Father, expose all enemies that are masquerading as my friends, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Every mountain of impossibility in my life, be uprooted and destroyed, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every ancestral wickedness, troubling my Israel, be cancelled, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every marine, terrestrial or celestial altar, assigned to pull me down, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Every spirit of shame, humiliation and embarrassment, assigned to police my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Every incantation and enchantment, released to keep me stagnant, be cancelled by the power in the blood of Jesus.

  9. Every spiritual gang up of the wicked spirit, assigned against my life, scatter by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Every strongman, supervising my demotion, die, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Conspiracies of darkness, assigned to remove my glory, shatter to pieces, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Every cooperation between household wickedness and work-place wickedness, to displace me, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Arrows of hatred and rejection, fired at me, go back to your senders, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Every satanic opposition and programme, assigned to pull me down, I de-programme you, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Every enemy, that pretends to be my friend to reduce me, I cut you off by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Every hater of my star and destiny, I command you to destroy yourself, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Every witchcraft power, assigned to manipulate me into downfall, I command your power to be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Every obstacle, on my way to glory, be evacuated, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Caterpillars and cankerworms, assigned to reduce me into nothing, die by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Every satanic bird, eating the seed of my destiny, I put you to flight, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    I decree and declare that no power of hell shall hold me down, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Every invisible coffin, that has swallowed my star, vomit it now, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Every arrow of Delilah and Jezebel, go back to your senders, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Every monitoring gadget and remote controlling devices, around me, break into pieces, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Every cage, chain and bag of witchcraft, fashioned against me, be destroyed, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Thou river of life, flow into every aspect of my life, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    O Lord, let the law of increasing return, replace the law of diminishing return in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    I prophesy increase, in every area of my life where there was once decrease, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    I decree, an ocean of blessing into my life where there was once a pond of blessing, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    I prophesy, the spirit of advancement and increase to take over from the spirit of retardation and reduction in every area of mylife, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    O God, arise and set my feet on higher ground, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Jesus Christ, water of life, flow into my life and my handi- work, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    I break myself loose, from every bondage of rising and falling, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    I decree upon my life, my desert shall blossom and there shall be streams for me to drink in a desert, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    I prophesy over my life, my wilderness shall become a fruitful field and it shall become a forest of blessing, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    I decree, that the people that I do not know shall serve and favour me, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O thou, that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  38.    Every enemy, of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O God, arise and uproot, anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  40.    Fire of revival, fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
