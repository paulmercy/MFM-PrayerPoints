Praise worship

Scripture Reading - Exodus 14

Confession - Jeremiah 17:18
  1. Every power assigned to turn my glory to shame, die, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Everything that must go for me to move forward, go by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Herod, Goliath, Cain and Pharaoh in my company, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Words of witchcraft, attacking my star, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Angels of war, pursue those holding what belongs to me and command them to release it, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every roaring lion that has vowed to devour me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Every instrument of wickedness assigned against me, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  8. The seed of the enemy shall not bear fruit, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Satanic garment of wretchedness, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Any witchcraft power working against my blessings, die, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    My stolen oil, hear the word of the Lord, gather back to my head, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Every power withholding my instruments of advancement, die, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Every evil hand pointed at my destiny, wither, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    My Father, give me personal Pentecost, give me fire to fight, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    My life, hear the word of the Lord, become a terror to the gates of hell, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Arrows of shame, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Every altar, planning my demotion, die, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Serpents and scorpions assigned to swallow my calling, die, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Where is the Lord God of Elijah, divide my Jordan by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Voices speaking against my rising up, shut up, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Jehovah, the story changer, arise and let my story change, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Powers behind strange battles, you are a liar, die, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Powers of delayed blessings, die, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Oh Lord, preserve my life from all sicknesses this year, and the days of my life, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Oh God, arise and give me divine power to produce wealth this year, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    This year, I shall not fall from the grace, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Oh Lord, I shall be surrounded by Your wall of fire throughout this year, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Every financial grave dug for me this year, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Every cloud of evil over my life, I blow you away, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Let the angels of God be installed as the gate keepers of my habitation, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    This year, satanic nightmare will not harm me, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    This year, every enemy of my life will stumble and fall, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    No divination, nor enchantment from evil men will work against my life and family, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  35.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
