Scripture Reading: Proverbs 4
Confession: Jeremiah 17:14

PRAYER POINTS

  1. Give thanks as the Holy Spirit leads you.

  2. Lord, cleanse me from every filthiness of the spirit.

  3. The Lord should promote me from minimum to maximum in all spheres of my life in Jesus name.

  4. I shall not labour in vain nor bring forth for trouble in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every good thing that my hands have laid its foundation shall be completed by my hands in Jesus name.

  6. I shall find favour in my going out and coming in the name of Jesus.

  7. I reject and denounce every spirit of failure.

  8. Lord, let holiness unto you be my watchword and my lot in the name of Jesus.

  9. I overthrow the citadel of sickness, weakness and fear in my life in the name of Jesus.

  10.   Let me be transfused with the Blood of the Lord Jesus Christ.

  11.   Lord, clothe me with the mantle of fire in Jesus name.

  12.   I reject every spiritual invitation to oppressors in my body in Jesus name.

  13.   Lord, make me your battle axe in the name of Jesus.

  14.   Lord, give me the grace to live in divine health.
