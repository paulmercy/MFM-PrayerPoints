Confessions: Psalms 5:19; Judges 13:1-25; Luke 2:25-32; Genesis 17:1-14; Genesis 21:1-7

PRAYER POINTS

  1. Sing songs of praises to the most High God because He is worthy of all praise.

  2. Exalt the name of God for His mighty works in your life.

  3. Praise His name because He is the ancient of days that cannot die.

  4. Unlimited mercy of God, overshadow me now, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Mercy of God and the light of God, appear in my life in the name of Jesus.

  6. Oh Lord, appear in my destiny and remould me for your glory in the name of Jesus.

  7. Oh Lord, appear in my life and marriage, let your goodness and joy spring forth, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Power of darkness, working against the manifestation of God’s power in my life, catch fire and burn to ashes in the name of Jesus.

  9. Every stumbling block that the enemy has placed on the path of my destiny and fruitfulness die, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Every evil ancient wall of darkness that surrounds me and is hindering good things from locating me, I pull you down by fire in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Every satanic battle, on assignment to block the Angel bringing my God’s given children, scatter in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Queen of the coast in conjunction with the prince of Persia that does not want me to receive the goodness and the answers to my prayers, catch fire and die, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Veil of darkness, covering my face in the spirit realm, blocking my helpers from locating me, catch fire in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Every dark cloud, covering my marriage and fruitfulness, clear away by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Heaven of my marriage and fruitfulness, open by fire in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Powers circulating my name for evil and deleting my testimonies die in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Every satanic battle on assignment to cause me to lose my opportunities and divine encounter, scatter in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Any power that needs to die, or run mad for me to dance and rejoice, die in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every battle that wants to strip me naked over my fruitfulness, catch fire and perish, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    I use the name of Jesus to conquer the evil intentions of occult and witchcraft powers saying no to my fruitfulness, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Oh Lord, show forth your mighty power, and disgrace powers of darkness that think they have completed their work over me, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    I use the stone of fire that killed Goliath to destroy every evil animal, eating up my pregnancy in the spirit realm, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Every organ in my life and that of my spouse, that has stopped working, receive the breath of life, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Every hindrance, obstacle, demonic report and evil prophecy that is saying I am not compatible with my spouse, thereby saying we will not have children, die in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Every evil elder’s meeting over my marriage and fruitfulness to spoil them, earthquake of God swallow them, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Everything that has being damaged in any part of my body, Oh Lord repair it, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Every evil padlock that has locked up my womb, break by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Oh God that located the sick man beside the pool of Bethsaida and healed him, locate me in the midst of the multitude, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Oh God that singled Mary out of other virgins and passed through her to show forth His glory, pass through me and show forth your glory, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Every dark strongman, that has put my life in the snare, I jump out of the snare and I put you there, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Oh Lord restore back to me all the years the devil has wasted concerning my fruitfulness, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Any covenant in my life that wants to waste my money and my properties, break and die, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    Strongman of darkness boasting as my God, that says I will not have breakthrough from this problem, be roasted by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    I trample upon………………………(pick from under the list) and you shall rise no more, in the name of Jesus.

    •   Every evil word that I shall die unfulfilled
    •   Every evil word that as I didn’t bring any child into my marriage, so shall I not have any in it
    •   Every evil word of witchcraft claiming ownership of my life
    •   Every evil word of “we have concluded his/her case and so shall it be”
    •   Every evil word of thou shall only hear good prophecies but you shall never see it manifest
    •   every evil word of bitterness, spoken into my life during my naming ceremony
    •   every evil word of bitterness spoken into my life on my wedding day that has become an evil guardian in my marriage
    •   every incantation and enchantment, spoken against my life and is now transforming into spiritual spouses
    •   evil words that spiritual robbers will rob me of my properties and children

  35.    Every evil word that his sperm will be too watery or her womb will be like basket leaking pregnancies.

  36.    Any evil prophecy that I shall conceive but I shall miscarry it or I shall give birth to babies and they shall die.

  37.    Every evil word that tears will not cease in my marriage and that I shall not laugh in my marriage.

  38.    Every evil word of thou shall get pregnant but shall die on the day of delivery.

  39.    Every evil word that my husband will die when I get pregnant.

  40.    Every evil word that once I get pregnant we shall loose our jobs and properties.

  41.    Every evil word, that I shall go insane during and after delivery, back fire in the name of Jesus.

  42.    The last menses will be the last blood I shall see till I deliver my baby in the name of Jesus.

  43.    I claim all the good prophecies in the scriptures in the name of Jesus.

  44.    As the Lord liveth and as His spirit is alive, I will continue to sing my song and dance my dance, in the name of Jesus.

  45.    There shall be no evil carry over and people shall not say sorry, you will make it next time to me in the name of Jesus.

  46.    I must be congratulated because a sound of joy is coming on my way, in the name of Jesus.

  47.    LORD I thank you for singling me out for blessing.
