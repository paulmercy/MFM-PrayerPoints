Scripture Reading - 1 Cor.15

Confession - Psalm 118:17

Praise worship
  1. I bind and cast out every graveyard spirit harassing my life, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Any soul tie with any dead relative, break, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every demonic wind stirred up against me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Every sickness released against me from the grave yard, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Blood of Jesus, destroy every ritual made against me in the grave yard, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Voice of the Lord, thunder and fire, destroy every mystery of darkness working against me, in the name of Jesus.

  7. I destroy the association of witches and native doctors assigned against me, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Gates of the grave assigned against me, be closed by the blood of Jesus.

  9. Any power calling my name from the gates of grave, die, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Every evil decree issued against my destiny, be revoked by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Every influence of grave yard spirits over my life, break, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Every evil transmission from the grave, clear away, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Voices from the grave, be silenced, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Serpents and scorpions assigned against me from the grave, die, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Every tongue raining incantations against me, dry up, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Let the sharp teeth of the enemy turn against the enemy, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    If I have left my place of blessings, O Lord, chase me back there, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    If my blessings have left me, O Lord, chase them back to me, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    O God that appeared unto Israel in the land of affliction, manifest Your power, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Every power troubling my star, be troubled, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Altar of wickedness, be desolated by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Rod of God’s anger, arise against the troublers of my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    All ancestral debt-collector forcing me to pay for what I did not buy, die, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Network of witchcraft in my place of birth, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Every power scheming to waste my life, receive the baptism of destruction, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    With Your hammer of fire, O God, arise and break the jaw of the oppressor, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Every evil hand of the enemy upon my star, wither, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    In this year, I cross from Egypt to Canaan, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Angels of the living God, minister destruction and confusion unto them that obstruct my progress, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    O dry bones of my greatness scattered in witchcraft valleys, gather and live, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    My sleeping giant, arise and fulfill your God-given destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Those that despise me in the past, shall seek my favour, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    Every foundational altar speaking against my destiny, die, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    Let the womb of this year deliver my breakthroughs, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    Every attack against my destiny when I was a child, be destroyed, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    I cancel the effect of any evil hand that carried me as a baby, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    Every satanic investigation into my future, be dismantled, in the name of Jesus.

  38.    O Lord, give me the achievements that will swallow my past failures, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  40.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in Jesus’ name.

  41.    O God arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries.

  42.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
