Praise worship

Scripture Reading - 1 Samuel 17

Confession - Isaiah 43:2

PRAYER POINTS:

  1. Let double destruction fall upon the enemy of my destiny in the name of Jesus.

  2. Let the fire of God arise with its weapons and pursue my pursuers in the name of Jesus.

  3. O Lord, turn me to coals of fire that no strange fire can overcome in the name of Jesus.

  4. I receive power to put all the enemies of my breakthroughs to flight in the name of Jesus.

  5. I shall not die in the wilderness of life in the name of Jesus.

  6. I refuse to rotate and circulate on the same spot in the name of Jesus.

  7. I withdraw my name from the register of frustrated people in the name of Jesus.

  8. As the Lord liveth every step I take as from now on will lead me to my destiny in the name of Jesus.

  9. I revoke every satanic decree against my advancement in the name of Jesus.

  10.    I release confusion into the camp of my enemies who are planning against my forward march in the name of Jesus.

  11.    I bind every spirit that is prolonging my stay in the wilderness of life in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Every Lot in my journey pack your load and go in the name of Jesus.

  13.    I raise the standard of the blood of Jesus against the spirit of vagabond in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Every chain tying me down to the same spot of frustration and poverty break now in the name of Jesus.

  15.    I receive the anointing for victory over the spirit of backwardness in the name of Jesus.

  16.    I destroy every satanic program targeted against my star in the name of Jesus.

  17.    I shall not tarry in the wilderness of confusion in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Every Red Sea confronting me on my way to the Promised Land divide by fire in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Today, I enroll as a candidate in the school of signs and wonders in the name of Jesus.

  20.    I receive power to run through a troop and leap over the wall in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Every Lazarus of my destiny hear the voice of resurrection and arise in the name of Jesus.
