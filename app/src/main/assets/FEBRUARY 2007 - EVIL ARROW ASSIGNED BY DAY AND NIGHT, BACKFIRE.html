Praise worship

Scripture Reading Psalm 118

Confession - Deut. 7:15
  1. Every evil against me, be judged today, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Every power assigned to put ashes on my head, die, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every evil throne reigning against me, be scattered, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Every satanic agent assigned against me, be smitten, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every power assigned to put me on the sick bed, be disgraced, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every chariot of the enemy assigned against me, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  7. I come out of every slavery and chains, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I cast down evil powers and strongholds and set them on fire, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Every man contesting against the oil on my head, loose your power, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   Arrows of the wicked, be diverted and backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   Covenant oil of God, fall upon me, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   I lift away every garment of darkness, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   Curses by night and day shall not stand, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   Arrow of satanic judgement, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   Everything the enemy has stolen by day or night, come back, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   Arrows assigned by day, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   Arrows assigned by night, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   Arrows that stole from me, be broken by the power of God, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   Every altar that has risen against me by day, break, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   Every altar that has risen against me by night, break, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   Incantations from witchcraft opposition, clear away, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   O God, arise and set the altars of the wicked on fire, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   Every mouth of the wicked speaking against me shut up, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   Every power stealing my promotion, die, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   Every abnormal pattern in my family, break away, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   Every confusion holding me captive, break away, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   The yoke of the destroyer shall not prosper, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   Ministry of fear in my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   Let my portions come from heaven, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   O Lord, show my soul mercy, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   Strange spiritual garments, fall away from my body, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   You tormenting-affliction, the Lord rebuke you, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   Like the rising of the sun, O God of wonders, arise in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   The glory of my rising shall not diminish, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   Power of God, consume every hindrance to my breakthroughs, in the name of Jesus.

  36.   Stubborn yokes assigned to my life, fall away, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   Anxiety assigned to my life, fall away, in the name of Jesus.

  38.   Evil burdens assigned to my life, fall away, in the name of Jesus.

  39.   Divine restoration, arise and locate me, in the name of Jesus.

  40.   O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  41.   Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in Jesus’ name.

  42.   O God arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries.

  43.   Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
