Bible Reading: Psalm 46: 1-11, 2: 1-4, 35: 1-8, 126: 1-6, Deuteronomy 28: 1-14

Confession: Matthew 21: 44, Psalm 57.

  1. I thank you God that I can see this beautiful day, in the name of Jesus.

  2. God I thank you because you did not allow the battles of life to overcome me, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Mercy of God, turn my portion around for good, in the name of Jesus.

  4. I soak my life and my environment in the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Let the heavens of my prayer open and let the presence of God come down, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every strong battle confronting me right from the first night in my marriage, die, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Battles that entered my life during my honeymoon and it’s still operating in my life, get out by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  8. The first food I ate after joining my husband, that is causing problem in my life, come out with your root, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Every stubborn battle that has taken the garment of glory and garment of fruitfulness from me, die and let my garments be restored, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Every stubborn battle exposing my nakedness as regards childbearing in the spiritual and physical realm, die, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Every strong battle that says my life will be stagnant like pond water, break and die, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Every stubborn battle that says I will not be seen or my voice heard among the fruitful ones, break and die, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    You strong battle that has taken laughter away from my life, enough is enough, die, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Every strong battle caused by curses and spells, that says my life will not spring forth joy and happiness, break and die, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Battles causing the fountain of my life to bring forth bitterness like marah water, die by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Battles assigned to prevent God’s promises for me year from coming to pass, break and die, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Battles and its owner, that has vowed to die with me, I am not ready to die now, die by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Vehicle of battle that I am inside in the spirit that can cause damage to my life, O God arise, stop the vehicle and bring me out, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every battle of life that has swallowed me and my unborn children, vomit us and die, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Every battle that wants my life to be like a dry tree in the field, that cannot bring forth fruits, break and die, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Holy Ghost! confront with fire, every battle that is wasting the blood of my child bearing and my sperm, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Battles from my husband’s/wife’s family or my family that I cannot confront with machetes and guns, O God arise and fight for me, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Evil wicked elder, that I have come across in my life that has stolen something good from me, I summon you here, return what you have stolen from me and die, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    O God of Elijah, deliver my children from the battles that are tying them down in the heavenlies, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    O God of Elijah, deliver me from evil wicked mothers, that tied me down in the spirit realm like an animal and feeding me with grass, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Every witchcraft coven that raise up battle against my fruitfulness be roasted by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Every power that placed a curse on me, that I will have sexual intercourse with my husband / wife and have nothing to show for it, die, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Any strongman assigned to plant evil seeds beside the good seeds of God in my life, die by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    O God deliver me from any one I offended, that has refused to forgive me and is making my time melt away like candle, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    O God arise and fight the hidden household enemy’s battle, that confront my life making me to cry every month, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    O God of Elijah, as you stopped the evil works of Jezebel for Jehu to become king, stop all evil works against me to become fruitful, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    O God put to open shame, all enemies of my marriage and fruitfulness and wipe away my tears, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    By the finger of God, let every sickness, hidden in my organs, system, intestine etc come out and die, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    Lord Jesus by your mighty finger, the blind received their sights, touch my womb / testes to come alive for conception, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    Let God be God in my situation and let all enemies be disappointed with their evil works, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    By your visitation that removed filthy garment from Hezekiah, remove the filthy garment of barrenness from my life, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O God, whatsoever needs to be changed in my body, be it in my organs or systems, for my prayer point to change for good, O God arise and change it, in the name of Jesus.

  38.    Every evil charm tested in my body by wicked elders, that does not want me to be fruitful till I enter the grave, come out of my body and die, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    As I smite my stomach with the help of the Holy Spirit, let every evil thing put in my body by wicked elders come out by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  40.    Every evil arrow and bullet of the wicked hunter fired into my body in he dream, come out and die, in the name of Jesus.

  41.    Every anxiety neurosis (This is a state where mental exhaustion is caused by stress, it signifies negative thinking and behaviours) projected into my life because of delay in childbearing depart from me now, in the name of Jesus.

  42.    Heart palpitation (It is the sensation when your heart skipped a beat or added an extra beating) and fear that has been projected into my life, my life is not your candidate, die, in the name of Jesus.

  43.    Every psychosomatic symptom (a physical disease that is thought to be caused or made worse by mental factors) in my body, die, in the name of Jesus.

  44.    Every visual or auditory hallucination projected into my life, vanish, in the name of Jesus.

  45.    Every panic attack that the enemy has programmed into my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  46.    Every spirit of depression, frustration and despondency programmed into my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  47.    Spirit of envy and jealousy programmed into my life in order to stop me from rejoicing, when God blesses others, die, in the name of Jesus.

  48.    Spirit of anger causing enmity between me and God, depart from my life now, in the name of Jesus.

  49.    Spirit of desperation, that makes me seek help from Egypt, die, in the name of Jesus.

  50.    I command the Hypothalamus-pituitary Axis (i.e. the central stress response system or body’s stress system) in my brain to function well and I programme my body for conception by the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.

  51.    Blood of Jesus, flush out every anti-conception hormone in my body, in the name of Jesus.

  52.    Eggs for multiple conceptions (twins, triplets), enter into my womb in the name of Jesus.

  53.    O God I thank you for wasting all wasting battles, assigned against my life in the name of Jesus.
