Scripture Reading - Judges 5

Confession -Isaiah 8:10
  1. Let the laws behind stubborn walls in my life be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Every law and ordinance assigned to be tormenting me, I command you to fail, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every law contrary to the word of God assigned against my life, I command you to break, in the name of Jesus.

  4. You tormentors assigned against my life, be dismantled from my life, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every Senacherib assigned against my life, fall by your sword, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Let the fire of God answer the fire of the enemy assigned against my life, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Let the God that answers by fire answer my prayers today and let Him be my God, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Let the enemies that do not want to let me go be electrocuted, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Let the hunters of God deal with every witchcraft power assigned against my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Let the wasters of God waste the powers assigned to trouble my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    O Lord, release fire to go before me and burn every power assigned to be challenging my soul, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    By the power that created the heaven and the earth, I will become God’s promotion, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Let every principle of heaven for my life begin to take root, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    O God, arise and protect my portions in this world, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Any evil conjured against my destiny, vanish, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Let the glory of God speak for me today, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    By the power in the blood of Jesus, I oppress the wicked, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    O Lord, release unto me the angels of power, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Let the power of God establish my promotion, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    O God arise and change my identity, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    O God arise, and honour me with the provision of heaven, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    O God arise, and let every witchcraft in my household scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    I set the judgment of God against every witchcraft altars assigned against my life, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Every good thing I touch will begin to prosper and become a harvest, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    I receive the mantle to harvest good things, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    O Lord, gather my portions from all the over the world unto me, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Let the altar of God shake up the camp of my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    My promotion shall not be mistakenly carried away with the wind, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    O Lord, fashion me unto useful vessels, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    O Lord, let my words carry divine fire, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Take Your place, O Lord, in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  33.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  35.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
