Scripture Reading: Psalm 91

Confession: Psalm 46

AGGRESSIVE PRAISE WORSHIP

  1. O God arise, and uproot anything You did not plant, inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  2. O God, let the fire of revival, fall upon the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O God, let the power of peace and progress, overshadow this nation, in the name of Jesus.

  4. O God, arise and give us God-fearing leaders, in Jesus’ name.

  5. Father, we confess and repent of the sins that opened the door for this pestilence, in the name of Jesus.

  6. We plead for mercy and forgiveness, in the name of Jesus.

  7. O Lord, forgive us for making You so angry, in the name of Jesus.

  8. We repent from all our iniquities, O Lord, in the name of Jesus.

  9. O Lord, let Your mercy speak and have mercy upon us as a nation, in Jesus’ name.

  10.   We sanitize our air with the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   O Lord, save our land from destruction and judgment, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   O Lord, let Your healing power begin to operate upon our land, in Jesus’ name.

  13.   We bind and cast out the crowned dragon from the East out of our land, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   Divine immunity, by the blood of Jesus, overshadow all our health workers, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   Power of the Most High, overshadow those seeking for cure and prevention, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   O Lord, grant our leaders divine wisdom to manage this crisis, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   You dark stronghold in charge of sickness and disease in our land, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   We use the blood of Jesus to sanitize our land and cleanse it from every coven-sponsored sickness and disease, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   Thou dragon power behind this pestilence, we bury you forever by the power in the blood of Jesus, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   Every wicked spirit, assigned to terminate my life, perish and to melt away like wax in the fire, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   Thunder fire of God, scatter, destroy, and break into pieces every dark gathering against my existence, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   I decree that the evil plots, evil devices, and evil counsels of the wicked against my destiny shall not stand; neither shall they come to pass,in the name of Jesus.

  23.   Every evil weapon, formed against my existence, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   The enemy assigned to come against me while I sleep, take your message back to your sender in sevenfold, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   The wall of the blood of Jesus is around me and my household, any evil force that comes near us shall catch fire, in Jesus’ name.

  26.   Lord, let the power base of this pandemic in the heavenlies and the waters be broken to pieces, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   O great wind from the wilderness, arise, locate the base of this pandemic, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   Lord, let every killer disease believing they have grown beyond the reach of destruction and judgement be destroyed, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   Blood of Jesus, laminate everyone in this country and protect them from death, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   We decree that our people shall dwell inside the envelope of divine fire and become untouchable by any power of infirmity, in the nameof Jesus.

  31.   O wind of God, arise and sweep away the scourge from our land, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   We pull down every stronghold of this pandemic, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   O Lord, let Your healing power begin to operate upon our land, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   By the power in the blood of Jesus, we raise an altar of victory, praise, worship of Jehovah in the land and nations, in Jesus’ name.

  35.   We invite the Lordship of the Lord Jesus Christ and His dominion over our neighbouring cities and nations, in the name of Jesus.

  36.   By the power in the blood of Jesus, we fumigate and cleanse our air with the blood of Jesus and we command death in the atmosphere todepart, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   We command the air to vomit all the seeds and tokens of death it has haboured, in the name of Jesus.

  38.   Passover blood of Jesus, envelope our nation, in the name of Jesus.

  39.   Passover blood of Jesus, envelope our land, in the name of Jesus.

  40.   Passover blood of Jesus, envelope our environment, in the name of Jesus.

  41.   Passover blood of Jesus, envelope our habitation, in the name of Jesus.

  42.   Passover blood of Jesus, envelope our neighbourhood, in the name of Jesus.

  43.   Passover blood of Jesus, envelope me and my family, in the name of Jesus.
