Scripture Reading - Psalm 27

Confessions - Psalm 125:3

Make these confessions violently. The word of God concerning my life is settled in heaven. I bind myself to the eternal word of God. I decree that any dream, word, vision, that did not originate from God are cancelled in my life. Every tree my heavenly Father has not planted, I uproot them, in the name of Jesus. I fire back, every spiritual bullet fired against me in my dreams. I surround myself with the fire of God. No power of darkness will have a way into my life again. My Father, let Your heavenly hosts and Your warriors, take over my battles. Oh Lord of hosts, arise and fight for me. I arise and put on the whole armour of God and I resist every power of darkness. Every power assigned to manipulate my life, I crush you, in the name of Jesus. Every evil spirit that has attacked me in my dream, I command you to be disgraced, in the name of Jesus. By the power in the blood of Jesus, be bound and overthrown.

  1. I renounce every conscious and unconscious familiar spirit and I reject your covenant, in the name of Jesus.

  2. You the spirit of grave and death, be scattered, in the name of Jesus.

  3. I cancel every curse of untimely death sent against me, in the name of Jesus.

  4. I refuse to die before my time, in the name of Jesus.

  5. I plead the blood of Jesus over my body, soul and spirit and neutralise every satanic poison deposited in my life.

  6. Sickness, pain and weakness, get out of my system, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Every disease, planted into my body, through dreams, get out, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Every effort of the enemy to weaken my prayer life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  9. I cancel every dream of hopelessness, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    I banish every senseless and meaningless dream, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Negative dreams shall not come to past in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    You spiritual cannibals, vomit my blood and flesh now, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    You evil night workers, I crush your powers, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Every spirit that has been oppressing me in my dreams, I declare my house and room danger zones for you, in Jesus’ name.

  15.    You the powers of the night, I command the power of God to pursue you, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    You the powers of the night, I command the sword of the Lord to slay you, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Every evil power of the ministers of the night, be dismantled, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Every cord of the powers of the night, be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every snare of the powers of the night, be destroyed, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Every evil control of the powers of the night, be destroyed, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Every evil bondage of the powers of the night, break, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Let every work of the powers of the night be exposed and brought to the light, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Thou evil powers of the night, be humbled and disgraced, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Let the evil throne of the powers of the night be dismantled by the thunder power of God, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Every magical operation of the night, be destroyed, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Every enchantment and witchcraft powers of the night, die, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    I heap shame and vengeance of the Lord upon every evil power of the night, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    All curses of the evil powers of the night, be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Every evil soul tie that is influencing me with the evil powers of the night, be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Every door opened by the evil powers of the night, be closed, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    I attack and destroy all areas of control held by the evil powers of the night, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Quencher of deliverance, die, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    I cancel all plans and programmes of the evil powers of the night, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   I de-programme every assignment of the evil powers of the night, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  36.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in Jesus’ name.

  37.    O God arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries.

  38.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
