Praise and worship

Bible Reading: 1 Sam 1: 1-20

Confessional Text: Luke 1: 37 “For with God nothing shall be impossible.”

PRAYER POINTS:

  1. Holy Ghost and fire, breathe into all the organs of my body, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Anything in my body that is dead, come alive by the power of God, in the name of Jesus.

  3. My womb, I command you to receive divine fruitfulness, in the name of Jesus.

  4. By the power that brought forth Lazarus from the grave, let my children come forth, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Affliction programmed into my body, die, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every evil growth, in my womb, die, in the name of Jesus.

  7. By the power that fertilized the womb of Hannah, let all my reproductive organs receive fertilization, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Call your name (for example Rebekah), you shall not be barren, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Every curse of barrenness operating in my life, be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   Every hindrance to my child bearing, be roasted by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   Every spirit spouse attacking my husband/wife, die, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   Demonic lion roaring against my conception, die, in Jesus’ name.

  13.   I command fruitfulness into my life, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   Activities of spirit spouse to scatter my marriage, scatter, in Jesus’ name.

  15.   Let the glory of God, cloud of tire, overshadow my marriage, in, Jesus’ name.

  16.   Any organ of my body that has been demonically exchanged, I collect you back by tire, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   Oh God of power, appear and remove the shame of my youth, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   Every satanic cloud hindering my earthly children, be roasted, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   By tire by thunder, the joy of my enemy over my life, be terminated, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   Every arrow of inherited barrenness in my life, die, in Jesus’ name.

  21.   Every spiritual hand terminating my pregnancy, wither, in Jesus’ name.

  22.   Every curse pronounced upon my marriage, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   Every dry organ in my body, receive the life of God and come alive, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   Every pattern of barrenness in my life, break, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   Evil power of my father’s house, release my body now, in Jesus’ name.
