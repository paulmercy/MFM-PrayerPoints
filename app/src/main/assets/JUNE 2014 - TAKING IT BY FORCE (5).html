Scripture Reading: 1 Samuel 17

Confession: Matthew 11:12

Aggressive Praise Worship
  1. My name, my virtue, jump out of every evil location, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Any creature or location holding my destiny and glory, release them and die, in the name of Jesus.

  3. My buried blessings, resurrect by fire in the name of Jesus.

  4. Any serpent holding my destiny, vomit them now and die, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Strange gods, assigned against me, scatter by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Evil loads, assigned against me, go back to the sender, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Celebrations of the enemies, over my life, turn to lamentation, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Laughter of my enemies, turn to tears, in the name of Jesus.

  9. I uproot every sickness and diseases from my body now, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    My adversaries shall end in chaos, shame and confusion, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Holy Ghost, bulldoze obstacles from my pathway, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Every plan of demotion, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    O heavens, arise, thunder against my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    O God of Elijah, arise and make me a mysterious wonder, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    O heavens, thunder against all demons enforcing curses in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Holy Ghost fire, unload me, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Every power assigned to turn my glory to shame, die in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Every power challenging my destiny, die in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every power mocking my God, die in the name of Jesus.

  20.    I move from glory to glory, from strength to strength, from grace to grace, from favour to favour by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Every evil power using my glory to shine, die in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Miracles that will wipe out my past ridicule, manifest, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    I shall not see shame, I shall not see demotion, I shall not see defeat, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Uncommon and extraordinary testimony, locate me, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    My Father, do what will make me to forget past years of pain, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Every Jordan of stagnancy and frustration, divide now, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    My Father, empower my angels of blessings to breakthrough now, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Powers planting curses in my star, die, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Any problems in my life, caused by using my hair as a baby, die, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Dark powers, supervising my problems, die, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Powers planted in my childhood, to trouble my future, hear the word of the Lord, die, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Satanic investments in the foundations of my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    Every witchcraft power, tracing my star, I bury you now, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    I release my star from voodoo or fetish powers, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    Woe unto every power assigned to stagnate me, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    Let the candle of the wicked be put out, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  38.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  40.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
