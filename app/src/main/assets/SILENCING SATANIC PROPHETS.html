Praise worship

Scripture Reading - 1 Kings 18

Confession - Deut. 33:26-27

PRAYER POINTS

  1. O God of Elijah, arise in Your anger and scatter every gathering of evil prophet assigned against me, in the name of Jesus.

  2. I receive the anointing to disgrace every utterance of darkness assigned to derail me, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every prophet of Baal of my father’s house contending with my destiny, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Where is the Lord God of Elijah, arise and disgrace my disgrace, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every yoke of bewitchment, be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every tongue anointed to waste my destiny, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  7. My Father, arise with Your sword of fire and cut down any evil tree growing against me, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Every arrow fired against my brain, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Killer covens assigned against me, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Wherever satanic agents are gathered against me, your time is up, be scattered unto desolation, in Jesus name.

  11.    Where is the Lord God of Elijah, arise, pursue my pursuers, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Where is the Lord God of Elijah, arise, divide my Jordan, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Where is the Lord God of Elijah, arise and make me a mysterious wonder, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Where is the Lord God of Elijah, arise and make my way plain before my face, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Where is the Lord God of Elijah, arise and empower me for uncommon breakthroughs, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Where is the Lord God of Elijah, arise and scatter every Jezebel assigned against me, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Every Ahab power pursuing my internal prophet, die, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Any power pressing my head down, I shake you into the fire of God, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Satanic agents assigned to stop me, I stop you before you stop me, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Satanic coma or full stop assigned to detain me, vanish, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    My hidden treasures buried in secret, come forth now, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Opportunity aborters, I puncture your powers, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Every altar of satanic delay, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Yokes assigned to frustrate my efforts, break, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Where is the Lord God of Elijah, move me forward by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Oil of favor from heaven, baptize my head, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Evil fingers pointed against my destiny, wither, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Any invisible chain on my legs, break, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Anointing for victory laughter, fall upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Barriers and strongholds erected to stop me, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Anti-progress enchantments and divination, backfire, in the name of Jesus.
