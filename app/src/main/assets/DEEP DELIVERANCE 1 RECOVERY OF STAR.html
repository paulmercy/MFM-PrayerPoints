Scripture Reading: Genesis 41

Confession: Psalm 138:8

PRAYER POINTS
  1. My star, arise, shine and fall no more, in Jesus’ name.

  2. My star, be invisible in the spirit realm, to every dark observer, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Unbroken curses, troubling my star, break, in Jesus’ name.

  4. Every evil hand, upon my star, wither, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Words of witchcraft, attacking my star, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every power of Herod, tracing my star, I bury you now, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Every mountain of difficulty, confronting my star, be levelled to the ground, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Every aggression, against my star, die, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Any power, redirecting my star, die, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Blood of Jesus, make it impossible for the enemy, to track down my star in the realm of the spirit, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    I shall become a star, in my generation, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Every power, troubling my star, be troubled, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Every evil hand of the enemy, upon my star, wither, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Star hunters, pursuing my star, dry up, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Every arrow of backwardness, fired into my star, die, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Chain of delay, holding my star, break, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Glory of the later rain, overshadow my star, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    My star, manifest by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every arrow of evil delay, fired into my star, die, in Jesus’ name.

  20.    Every demonic blanket, that has covered my star, I tear you and destroy you, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    My star, rise and shine clearly, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Birds of darkness, assigned to trouble my star, die, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    My star be honoured in the land of the living, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    My star, arise, and shine; no power shall stop you, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Every cord of witchcraft holding down my star, break, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Arrows, fired into my star to demote me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Every assignment of dark hunters, for my star, wither, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    O Lord, let the stars fight, against every witchcraft bird, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Thou evil cloud, overshadowing my star, clear away, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Every satanic arrow, fired at my star, fall down and die, in Jesus’ name.

  31.    Plantations of darkness, troubling my star, roast by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Power to interpret the language of my star, come upon me now, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    Power to read the handwriting of my star, in the heavenlies, fall upon me, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    You dragon tracing my star, I rebuke you, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    My star, appear, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    Powers, assigned to capture my star, loose your hold, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O thou, that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  38.    Every enemy, of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O God, arise and uproot, anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  40.    Fire of revival, fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
