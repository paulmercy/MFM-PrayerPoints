Praise worship

Scripture Reading - Daniel 2

Confession - Daniel 2:22
  1. O Lord, give unto me the Spirit of revelation and wisdom in the knowledge of Yourself.

  2. O Lord, make Your way plain before my face on this issue.

  3. O Lord, reveal to me every secret behind any problem that I have.

  4. O Lord, bring to light every thing planned against me in darkness.

  5. I remove my name from the book of those who grope and stumble in darkness in the name of Jesus.

  6. O Lord, make me a vessel capable of knowing Your secret things.

  7. Every ancestral secret retarding my progress, be revealed, in Jesus’ name.

  8. Evil secret activities currently affecting my life, be exposed and disgraced, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Every secret I need to know to excel spiritually and financially, be revealed, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Every secret hidden in the marine kingdom, affecting my elevation, be exposed and disgraced, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Every secret hidden in the satanic archive, crippling my elevation, be exposed and disgraced, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Every secret I need to know about my environment, be revealed, in Jesus’ name.

  13.    Every secret I need to know about my father’s lineage, be revealed, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Every secret I need to know about my mother’s lineage, be revealed, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Every secret I need to know about my hometown, be revealed, in Jesus’ name.

  16.    Every secret I need to know about the work I am doing, be revealed, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Oh Lord give unto me the Spirit of revelation and wisdom in the knowledge of Yourself.

  18.    Oh Lord, make your way plain before my face on this issue.

  19.    Oh Lord, remove spiritual cataract from my eyes.

  20.    Oh Lord, reveal to me every secret behind the particular issue whether beneficial or not.

  21.    Holy Spirit, reveal deep and the secret things to me about ..., in Jesus’ name.

  22.    I bind every demon that pollutes spiritual vision and dreams, in Jesus’ name.

  23.    I receive power to operate with sharp spiritual eyes that cannot be deceived, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Divine revelations, spiritual visions, dreams and information will not become scarce commodity in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    O God, dash the power of stubborn pursuers in pieces like a potter’s vessel, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    O God, arise with all Your weapons of war and fight my battle for me, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    My Father, be a shield for me in every situation, in Jesus’ name.

  28.    I will not be afraid of ten thousands of people that have set themselves against me, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    My Father, break the teeth of the ungodly, in Jesus’ name.

  30.    O God, visit every power lying against me with destruction, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Let my enemies fall by their own counsel, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Cast out my enemies in the multitude of their transgressions, in Jesus’ name.

  33.    Let all my enemies be ashamed and sore vexed, in Jesus’ name.

  34.    Let sudden shame be the lot of all my oppressors, in Jesus’ name

  35.    Every power planning to tear my soul like a lion, be dismantled, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    O God, command judgement on all my oppressors, in Jesus’ name.

  37.    O Lord, let Your anger boil against the wicked every day, in the name of Jesus.

  38.    Father, fight against them that fight against me, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O God, prepare the instruments of death against my enemies, in Jesus’ name.

  40.    O God, ordain Your arrows against my persecutors, in the name of Jesus.

  41.    Let every pit dug by the enemy become a grave for the enemy, in Jesus’ name.

  42.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  43.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  44.    O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  45.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
