CONFESSION: Galatians 3:13-14, 2 Tim. 4:18, Col.1:13, Col.2:15.

PRAYER POINTS

  1. Thank God for making provision for deliverance from any form of bondage.

  2. Confess your sins and those of your ancestors, especially those sins linked to evil powers.

  3. Let the power in the blood of Jesus separate me from the sins of my ancestors.

  4. Begin to cover yourself with the blood of Jesus.

  5. I release myself from any inherited bondage and limitations, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Lord, send Your axe of fire to the foundation of my life and destroy every evil plantation.

  7. Thou power of God, begin to move in my life now and deliver me where I need deliverance, heal me where I need healing, and transform me where I need transformation.

  8. Let the blood of Jesus flush out from my system every inherited satanic deposit, in the Name of Jesus.

  9. I release myself from the grip of any problem transferred into my life from the womb, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    Blood of Jesus, fire of the Holy Ghost, cleanse every organ in my body, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    I break and loose myself from every inherited evil curse in the name of Jesus.

  12.    I break and loose myself from every inherited evil covenant, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    I vomit every evil consumption that I have been fed with, as a child, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    I command all foundational strongmen attached to my life to be paralyzed, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Let any rod of the wicked rising up against my family line rendered impotent for my sake, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    I cancel the consequences of any evil local name attached to my person, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    You evil foundational plantation, come out of my life with all your roots, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    I break and loose myself from every form of demonic bewitchment, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every satanic transfer into my destiny through the placenta, be cut off in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Every cage of placental manipulation, break, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Everything stolen from me through placenta manipulation, I repossess you now in Jesus name.

  22.    Thou womb of the earth using my placenta as a chain, release me in Jesus name.

  23.    Every power utilizing my placenta to summon my spirit, I break your power, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    I release myself from every evil domination and control, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    Let every gate opened to the enemy by my foundation be closed forever, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Let the blood of Jesus remove any unprogressive label from every aspect of my life.

  27.    Lord, create in me a clean heart by Your Power, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Lord, renew a right spirit within me, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Lord, ignite my calling with Your fire, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Lord, establish me as a holy person unto You, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Lord, restore my spiritual eyes and ears in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Lord, let the anointing to excel in spiritual and physical life fall on me.

  33.    O lord, let the anointing of the Holy Spirit break every yoke of backwardness in my life.

  34.    Lord Jesus, increase daily in my life.

  35.    Begin to thank God for the deliverance.
