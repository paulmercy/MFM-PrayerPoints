Scripture Reading: Psalm 149

Confession: Psalm 25:1-2

Aggressive Praise Worship
  1. Any power, that is sleeping to harm me, you shall not wake up, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Wealth of my enemies, be transferred to me, in the name of Jesus.

  3. He that speaks against me shall not prosper, in Jesus’ name.

  4. Every dark power, intimidating my destiny, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  5. I wipe out infirmity from my life, in the name of Jesus.

  6. I offload every burden of darkness from me, in the name of Jesus.

  7. I destroy every caldron speaking against me, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Every power using the dust against me, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  9. No wizard in my business/career shall prosper again, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    I declare casualty against witches and wizards in my household, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Fire of God, carry your anger to my oppressors, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Devourers that will pursue my enemies, arise and pursue them, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Angels of God, gather your arrows and attack my problems, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Storm of God, gather against my problems, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    O God, arise and gather Your thunder against my problems and kill them, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    O God, arise, and let Your angels fight my battles today, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    I pluck out wickedness out of my environment, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    O God, arise and make me Your power axe on earth, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every strange garment on my spiritual body, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Altar of Baal in my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    I get out of the stomach of every evil spirit, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Every trap, prepared for me by the enemy, turn around and catch your owner, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    I command the baptism of madness and backwardness, to fall upon my stubborn enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    My testimony, will not be buried, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    I will not suffer what my parents suffered, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Seed of untimely death, in the foundation of my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Seed of greatness in my life, manifest, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Weeping and oppression of the night, expire, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    I command my portion to be released, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Decision and judgement of the council of hell against my life, I terminate you now, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    I receive my daily increase and enlargement, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    Darkness, shall have no portion in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    O God arise, and let every altar of darkness scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    O day of the Lord, arise against my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    I condemn every situation rising against me, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    I cancel every wicked judgment issued against me, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O thou, that troubleth the Israel of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today, inthe name of Jesus.

  38.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O God, arise and uproot, anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  40.    Fire of revival, fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
