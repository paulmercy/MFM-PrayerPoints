Scripture Reading - Psalm 149
Confession - Psalm 18:37

Praise worship
  1. O Lord, cleanse me in the living waters that flow from Your heart, in the name of Jesus.

  2. O Lord, surround me with Your holy light, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O Lord, let Your healing waters purify my family line, in the name of Jesus.

  4. I reclaim any territory that was handed over to satan, in the name of Jesus.

  5. O Lord, reveal any aspect of my life that as not pleasing, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Anyway that could give satan a foothold in my life, be wiped out, in the name of Jesus.

  7. All emissaries of satanic headquarters, I bind your power, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I break and dissolve all traps and diversions, in the name of Jesus.

  9. I sever the transmission of all satanic vows, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   I proclaim Jesus Christ Lord of all circumstances and difficulties in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   I bind and reject all familiar spirits and spirits of affliction, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   I bind and I reject all spirits of division and disruption, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   I bind and reject all interlocking spirits, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   I come against all satanic vows, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   I attack the stronghold of the enemy, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   I command the enemy to release my mind, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   O Lord, empower me to become the person You created me to be, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   O Lord, show me how to apply Your word in my life each day, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   O Lord, have the supreme place in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   O Lord, cleanse me from everything evil I have inherited from my ancestors, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   I refuse to have anything from satan in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   I break evil lines of inheritance by the blood of Jesus.

  23.   Anything that allows the fiery darts of the enemy to penetrate my armour, fall off, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   Every scheme and devise of satan against me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   You the strongman in charge of my destiny, I bury you today, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   Every seed of witchcraft in my root, die, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   Every pattern of family destruction, die, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   Every witchcraft presence in my dream, what are you waiting for, die, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   Every arrow of oppression, die, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   Mirrors of darkness, break, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Every cauldron assigned to cook my destiny, break, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   Anointing that brings miracles, fall on me, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   Yoke-developers, swallow your yokes, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   Let the tree of witchcraft growing around me dry up and die, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  36.   Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
