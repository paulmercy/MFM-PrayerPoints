Praise and Worship

Scripture Reading - 2 Kings 5

Confession - Matt. 15: 13
  1. Evil does not belong to me, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Whatsoever the Lord did not plant in my life, be uprooted by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Any power creating problems for me, be disbanded, in the name of Jesus.

  4. O Lord, arise and uproot the finger of the wicked man from my life.

  5. I destroy the mouth of the wicked around me, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Let the earth open and swallow my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Every hole that wants to swallow me, be destroyed now, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Let the earth open up and help me, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Evil clouds around my life, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   I will arise and shine, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   I have come to Zion, my destiny must change, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   O Lord, turn my family Into a generation of praise.

  13.   O Lord, hiss over my enemies.

  14.   O Lord, clothe my enemies with shame.

  15.   O Lord, let Your fire envelope me.

  16.   Everything that has decided not to forgive but to destroy me, let God respond by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   Let the four winds of the earth gather against my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   O Lord, release Your carpenters on my behalf.

  19.   O Lord, let fire from on high be released to consume everything You have not planted in my life.

  20.   Every devouring fire in my life, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   Let heavens smite my oppressors with the waster, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   O Lord, set Your sword against my situation.

  23.   Let the serpent of the Lord bite every hidden enemy, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   Every problem that has defied human wisdom, receive solution now, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   I rebuke the finger of man causing my problems in the name of Jesus.

  26.   Wherever my feet tread every spell shell break, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   I smite every situation that smites me, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   The enemy I see today, I shall see them no more, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   I uproot every satanic Investment in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   I break down every network of demonic machinery, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   Let the strength with which the Lord makes war fall upon me now, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   I smash every evil cauldron working against me to pieces, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   Every record about my life in evil cauldrons, be destroyed by the blood of Jesus.

  34.   Every witchcraft deposit planted in my body in my sleep, come out by fire in the name of Jesus.
