CONFESSION: Eph.4:8-10

Praise Worship

  1. I break and loose myself from every Spirit of the dog, in the name of Jesus.

  2. I release myself from every spirit pollution emanating from past sins of fornication and sexual immorality, in the name of Jesus.

  3. I release myself from every ancestral pollution, in Jesus’ name.

  4. I release myself from every dream pollution, in Jesus' name.

  5. I command every plantation of the day in my life to come out with all his roots, in Jesus' name.

  6. Every demon of the dog, working against my life be paralyzed and get gut of my life, in Jesus‘ name.

  7. Every demon of the dog assigned to my life, be bound, in Jesus’ name.

  8. Father Lord, let the rower of the dog, oppressing my life, receive the fire of God and be roasted, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Every inherited demon of the dog in my life, receive the arrows of fire, in the he name of Jesus.

  10.   I command every force of power of the dog to come against themselves, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   Father Lord, let every demonic stronghold build into my life by the spirit of the dog be putted down, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   Let every power of the dog, that has summoned my life be shattered to pieces, in Jesus’ name.

  13.   Let my soul be delivered from the forces of the dog, in Jesus' name.

  14.   Let the Lord God of Elijah arise with a strong hand against every spirit husband/wife and power of the dog, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   I break the hold of any evil power over my life, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   I nullify every effect of the bite of the dog upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   Every evil stronger and satanic deposit in my life, I command you to be paralyzed and get out of my life, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   Holy Ghost fire, purge my life completely, in Jesus‘ name.

  19.   I claim my complete deliverance from the spirit of fornication and sexual immorality, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   Let my eyes be delivered from lustfulness, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   As from today, my eyes, be controlled by the Holy Spirit, in Jesus' name.

  22.   Holy Ghost fire, fall upon my eyes and burn to ashes every evil force and satanic power controlling my eyes, in Jesus name.

  23.   I move from bondage to liberty in every area of my life, in Jesus name.
