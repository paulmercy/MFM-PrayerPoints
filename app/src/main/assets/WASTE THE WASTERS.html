Praise worship

Scripture Reading - Exodus 12

Confession - Isaiah 54:16-17
  1. The law behind stubborn walls in my life, be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Every law ordinance that is tormenting me, I command you to fail, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every law contrary to the word of God, I command you to break, in the name of Jesus.

  4. You tormentor, be dismantled from my life, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every Senacherib against me, fall by your sword, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Let the fire of God answer the fire of the enemy and let the God that answers by fire be God, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Let my enemies be electrocuted, in the name of Jesus.

  8. O Lord, let Your hunters deal with witchcraft in my foundation, in the name of Jesus.

  9. O Lord, let Your wasters waste my enemies, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    O Lord, let Your wasters fight my devourers, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    O Lord, release Your fire to go before me and burn the challenger of my soul, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    I will become God’s promotion, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Let every principle of heaven for my life take root, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    O Lord, protect my portions, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    I will prevail in the days of testing, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Any evil conjured against me, vanish, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    O Lord, let Your glory speak for me today, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    I oppress the wicked by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    O Lord, let my divine destiny appear; let perverted destiny disappear, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    I reject every satanic rearrangement of my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    I refuse to live below my divine standard, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Let the handwriting of household wickedness be rubbed off, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Every evil power with awareness of my destiny, be impotent, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    I paralyze every destiny pollutant, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    O Lord, send divine plagues upon my Pharaoh, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    O Lord, let not my name disappear from Your book of remembrance, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Every power trying to remove my name from the mind of my helpers, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    I crush every satanic lion rising against my success, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Any power planning to re-build my Jericho, fall down and die, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    O Lord, let Your mighty voice thunder against my enemies and throw them into confusion, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Let every satanic ambush be frustrated, in the name of Jesus.
