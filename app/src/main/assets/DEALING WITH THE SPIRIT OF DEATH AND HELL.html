PRAYER POINTS

  1. I renounce and break every death covenant that I have formed or which anyone has formed on my behalf in Jesus name.

  2. I remove the control of my life from the hands of any dead person in Jesus name.

  3. I stand against every covenant of sudden death in the name of Jesus.

  4. Every blessing of mine that has been buried under the ground or under any water, be released in Jesus name.

  5. I cancel my name from every death register in the name of Jesus.

  6. I stand against every form of tragedy in the name of Jesus.

  7. Every grave cloth over my life, be removed in Jesus name.

  8. All my potentials that have been destroyed, receive life in the name of Jesus.

  9. I stand against all powers that push a person to hell fire in the name of Jesus.

  10.   I cover my spirit, soul and body with the blood of Jesus.

  11.   I remove my name from the book of untimely death in the name of Jesus.

  12.   I remove my name from the book of tragedy in the name of Jesus.

  13.   I disarm any power that has made a covenant with the ground, water and wind about me, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   I revoke any conscious or unconscious covenant with the spirit of death in the name of Jesus.

  15.   Every power pursuing me with death, die in the name of Jesus.

  16.   I reject premature death, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   I bind the spirit of death and hell over my life, in the name of Jesus.
