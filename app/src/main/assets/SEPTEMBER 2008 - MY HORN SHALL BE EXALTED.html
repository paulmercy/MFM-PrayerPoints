Praise worship

Scripture Reading - Psalm 92

Confession - Psalm 92:10
  1. I break the yoke of backwardness assigned against my destiny, in the name of Jesus.

  2. O God, arise and give me a turn around miracle, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O God, arise and connect me to my helpers, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Coffins of darkness, assigned against me, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  5. O God, arise and open Your treasures unto me, in the name of Jesus.

  6. O God arise and give me open heavens, in the name of Jesus.

  7. After the order of Peter, O Lord, deliver me from the expectation of the wicked, in the name of Jesus.

  8. O Lord, make me a candidate of supernatural surprises, in the name of Jesus.

  9. By fire, by force, O God, launch me into my next level, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    O God arise and restore my past losses, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Thou power of God, disgrace my detractors, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Any evil meeting summoned against my destiny, receive confusion, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    Disgrace, hear the word of the Lord, go back to the sender, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Lord, contend with them that contend with me, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    Lord, let my generation celebrate me, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    Every arrow of mourning and sorrow, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    O God, arise, turn my past mistakes to miracles, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    O God, arise and increase the speed of my progress, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Lord, convert any pain in my life to gain, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    O Lord, give me my personal Pentecost. Give me fire to fight, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Every satanic plan to kill me before my time, die, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    Lord, make me a blessing to my generation, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    Every wicked tongue programmed to attack my destiny, dry up, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    My life, become a terror to the gates of hell, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    My name, receive divine immunity from evil, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    Every satanic plan to spoil my name, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Lord, make me a reference point to my generation, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    I cancel tears. I cancel premature deaths, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    Star hunters, pursuing my star, dry up, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    O God, arise and confound every secret enemy determined to make me weep, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Arrows of shame, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    My Father, my Father, my Father, let people know I serve a living God, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    I pull down the arrows of stagnation, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    Every arrow of failure targeted at my destiny, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    You dreams of demotion, die, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    Lord, before the end of this month, make me to dance, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    My Father, deliver me from strange battles, in the name of Jesus.

  38.    Every altar planning my demotion, I curse you, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    Satanic breakdown at the edge of my breakthroughs, clear away, in the name of Jesus.

  40.    My wasted years and efforts hear the word of the Lord, be converted to blessings, in the name of Jesus.

  41.    My buried potentials hear the word of the Lord, manifest by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  42.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  43.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in Jesus’ name.

  44.    O God arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries.

  45.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
