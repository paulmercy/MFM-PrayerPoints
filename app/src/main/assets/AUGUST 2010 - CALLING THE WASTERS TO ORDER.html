Scripture Reading - Nahum 1

Confession - Deut 28:13

Praise worship
  1. Let the teeth of the enemy over my life break, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Let every covenant with the earth against my life be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Let every covenant with the sun, moon and stars against my life, be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  4. Let every covenant with the waters against my life, be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every one who has accepted witchcraft for my sake, be disgraced, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Every power crippling against me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Oh Lord, release the rivers of life upon my dwelling this year, in the name of Jesus.

  8. I shall not be a spiritual casualty, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Oh Lord, take away my portions from casualty, in the name of Jesus.

  10.    My God, my life shall not be left with the wind, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    The meanderings of the enemy will not prosper in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  12.    Let the excesses of darkness be checked by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    I break the law of death over my life, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    Every covenant of death with this year, be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    I am invisible to aggressive elements, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    I destroy every magic in any house I have entered, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Every evil power that has established authority in my family, be dismantled, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Whenever I go, satanic agents will not steal my portions, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Every agent of darkness, assigned against my life, I command the sword of the Lord to rise against you, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    Every biting demon, be silenced, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    Let the oil lost in my life be returned, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    I pull down every throne of iniquity, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    God will find rest in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Let the wall of challenges against my breakthroughs be broken, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    My neck shall not be broken by territorial spirits, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   Blood, poured on the ground, will not eat me up, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    My portion is not with the dead, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Every medicine, poured on the ground, to subdue my life, I destroy it, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    I destroy the hand of every witchdoctor working against me, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    Any blood sacrifice, against me, let the life in the blood, be revoked to smite them, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Oh Life, that has been allowed to die through rituals against me, arise, and strangulate your killers, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    I quench every anger energised through this land against me, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    By the power in the blood of Jesus, I subjugate the power of witches, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    I destroy the alignment of witches, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  36.    Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  38.    Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
