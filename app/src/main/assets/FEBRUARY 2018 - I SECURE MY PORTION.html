Scripture Reading: Psalm 2
Confession: Psalm 2

AGGRESSIVE PRAISE WORSHIP

  1. O God arise, and uproot anything You did not plant, inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  2. O God, let the fire of revival, fall upon the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  3. O God, let the power of peace and progress, overshadow this nation, in the name of Jesus.

  4. O God, arise and give us God-fearing leaders, in Jesus’ name.

  5. O Lord, this year, bring me into favour with all those that will decide on my advancement, in the name of Jesus.

  6. O Lord, in this year, let there be a divine substitution that will move me ahead, in the name of Jesus.

  7. I reject the spirit of the tail and I claim the spirit of the head, in the name of Jesus.

  8. O Lord, transfer, remove or change any power that is bent on stopping my advancement this year, in the name of Jesus.

  9. O Lord, this year, smoothen my path to the top by Your hand of fire, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   I receive the anointing to excel above my contemporaries, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   Lord, catapult me into greatness as You did for Daniel in the land of Babylon, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   I bind every strongman assigned to hinder my progress this year, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   O Lord, despatch Your angels to roll away every stumbling block to my promotion, advancement and elevation this year, in Jesus’ name.

  14.   I receive the mandate to put every enemy of my breakthroughs to flight, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   I reject every spirit of doubt, fear and discouragement, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   I cancel all ungodly delays to the manifestations of my miracles, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   O Lord, hasten Your Word to perform it in every department of my life, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   I refuse to agree with the enemies of my progress, in the mighty name of Jesus.

  19.   Father Lord, in this year, let there be turbulence, re-arrangement, revision, re-organisation and re-routing of situations andcircumstances in order to give path to my desired miracles, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   I bind, plunder and render to nothing every anti-testimony, anti-miracle and anti-prosperity forces, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   The God who answered by fire and the God of Elijah, answer me by fire this year, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   The God who answered Moses speedily at the Red Sea, answer me by fire this year, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   The God who changed the lot of Jabez, answer me by fire this year, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   The God which quickeneth and calleth those things that be not as if they are, answer me by fire this year, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   The God of all comfort and joy, answer me by fire this year, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   Every foreign knee assigned to prevent the manifestation of my miracles this year, bow, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   Father Lord, let every evil force gathered against my breakthroughs this year be scattered, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   I reject every journey to backwardness, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   Father Lord, let every agent of shame working against me be paralyzed, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   I paralyze the activities of household wickedness over my life, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   Lord, give me power for maximum achievement this year, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   O Lord, give me confronting authority to achieve effortless results in all my endeavours this year, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   I break every curse of untimely death, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   Father Lord, fortify me with Your power to excel this year, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   O Lord, let the counter-movement of the Holy Spirit frustrate every evil device assigned against my breakthroughs, in Jesus’ name.

  36.   Lord, make my voice the voice of peace, the voice of deliverance, the voice of power and the voice of solution, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   Lord, give me divine direction that will propel me to greatness this year, in the name of Jesus.

  38.   Every power assigned to use my family to torment me, be paralyzed, in the name of Jesus.

  39.   Lord Jesus, give me an excellent spirit, in the name of Jesus.

  40.   Where is the Lord God of Elijah, arise judge every tongue assigned to condemn me this year, in the name of Jesus.
