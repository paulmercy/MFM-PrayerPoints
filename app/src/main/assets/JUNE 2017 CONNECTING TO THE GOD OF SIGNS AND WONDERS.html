Scripture Reading: Luke 5

Confession: Exodus 15:11

Aggressive Praise Worship
  1. Father, let signs and wonders be my lot, in Jesus’ name.

  2. Every obstacle in my life, give way to miracles, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Every frustration in my life, become a bridge to my miracles, in the name of Jesus.

  4. I hold the blood of Jesus against demonic delay of my miracles, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every stubborn problem, in my life, be buried, in Jesus’ name.

  6. You miracle hijackers, release my miracles, now, by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  7. My Father, arise by Your signs and wonders and visit my life, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Like the rising of the sun, O God of wonders, arise in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Wonder-working power of God, be released on my situations for signs and wonders, in the name of Jesus.

  11.    Holy Ghost fire, visit me with Your signs and wonders, in the name of Jesus.

  13.    O God, arise and hear me in the day of trouble, in the name of Jesus.

  14.    O God of sign and wonders, You are the heavenly surgeon, touch me by Your power today, in the name of Jesus.

  15.    I shall not die because of my problems, in the name of Jesus.

  16.    I shall not be disgraced because of my problems, in the name of Jesus.

  17.    Fire of God, begin to attack all miracle hijackers assigned against my life, in the name of Jesus.

  18.    Every curse and covenant of impossibility, over my life, break, in the name of Jesus.

  19.    Thou Goliath of impossibility, in my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  20.    I shall not die undiscovered, in the name of Jesus.

  21.    I shall not die unused and unsung, in the name of Jesus.

  22.    I shall not die uncelebrated and unmissed, in the name of Jesus.

  23.    I shall not die unfruitful and unfulfilled, in the name of Jesus.

  24.    Every good thing the enemy has swallowed in my life, be vomited, in the name of Jesus.

  25.    O God, arise and send me help from the sanctuary and strengthen me out of Zion, in the name of Jesus.

  26.    My rescue and deliverance, be announced from heaven, in the name of Jesus.

  27.    Before I finish praying these prayers, O Lord, let Your angels move into action on my behalf, in the name of Jesus.

  28.    Every prince of Persia and all territorial spirits around me, that are hindering the manifestation of God’s miracle in my life,scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  29.    I bind and cast out of my vicinity, all prayer and miracle blockers, in the name of Jesus.

  30.    You miracle hijackers, release my miracles now by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  31.    Every satanic umbrella preventing the heavenly showers of blessings from falling on me, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  32.    O God, arise and let my heavens open right now, in the name of Jesus.

  33.    I shall not give up, because I have believed to see the goodness of the Lord in the land of the living, in the name of Jesus.

  34.    O Lord, let my soul be prevented from death, my eyes from tears and my feet from falling, in the name of Jesus.

  35.    People will hear my testimonies and glorify the name of God in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  36.    My Father, let Your divine intervention in my life bring souls to the kingdom of God, in the name of Jesus.

  37.    O thou, that troubleth the Israel of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  38.    Every enemy, of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  39.    O God, arise and uproot, anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  40.    Fire of revival, fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
