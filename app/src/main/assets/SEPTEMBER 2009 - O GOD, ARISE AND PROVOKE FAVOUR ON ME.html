Praise worship

Scripture Reading - Psalm 46

Confession - Philp. 4:19
  1. My stones of prayer shall locate the unprotected forehead of my Goliath, in the name of Jesus.

  2. Today, the taproot of failure that is working against me, die, in the name of Jesus.

  3. Anointing of bitterness and sorrow targeted against my life, dry up, in the name of Jesus.

  4. O God, arise and overanswer my prayers today, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Angels of God, arise, pursue, capture and bring my helpers to me, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Fingers of Jehovah, terminate every power that says over his dead body will I be successful, in the name of Jesus.

  7. My brain, receive divine fertilizer of wisdom that will increase my greatness, in the name of Jesus.

  8. All obstacles on my way to greatness, die, in the name of Jesus.

  9. Every Haman assigned to destroy me, die in my place, in the name of Jesus.

  10.   My star, arise, and shine, no power shall stop you, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   Dark powers, hear the word of the Lord, release my virtues, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   My stolen oil, hear the word of the Lord, gather back to my head, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   I break every chain assigned against my breakthroughs, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   Every cloud of bewitchment, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   Witchcraft and wizard flying against me, crash land, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   Every power assigned to put me on the sick bed, die, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   Arrows of satanic judgment, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   I claim unexpected income by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   Every midnight enchantment against me, backfire, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   As a child of the Lion of Judah, I chase serpents and scorpions out of my life, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   Stagnation and failure, I bury you today, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   O God, arise and provoke favour on my behalf, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   Darkness within me, wither, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   Blood of Jesus, apply your medication upon my life, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   O Lord, purify my life by your daily visitation, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   O Lord, increase in me, the spirit of watchfulness and prayer, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   Teach me, O Lord, the way of understanding, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   Set me free, O Lord, from the tyranny of sin, in the name of Jesus.

  29.   O Lord, send Your angels to cleanse any unfruitful ground of my life, in the name of Jesus.

  30.   Cleanse me, heal me, and renew me by Your Spirit, O Lord.

  31.   Father, give me the love and persistence to cry out to You day and night, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  33.   Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in Jesus’ name.

  35.   Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
