Praise worship

Scripture Reading - Luke 1

Confession - Philippians 4:13
  1. Before I finished praying these prayers, oh Lord, let Your angels move into action on my behalf, in Jesus’ name.

  2. Every prince of persia and all territorial spirits around me, that are hindering the manifestation of God’s miracle in my life, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  3. I bind and cast out of my vicinity, all prayer and miracle blockers, in the name of Jesus.

  4. You miracle hijackers, release my miracles now by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  5. Every satanic umbrella preventing the heavenly showers of blessings from falling on me, catch fire, in the name of Jesus.

  6. Oh God, arise and let my heavens open right now, in the name of Jesus.

  7. Every obstacle in my life, give way to miracles, in the name of Jesus.

  8. Every frustration in my life, become a bridge to my miracles, in the name of Jesus.

  9. I decree by fire and thunder that I shall not die before the manifestation of my miracles, in Jesus’ name.

  10.   You miracle hijackers, release my miracles now by fire, in the name of Jesus.

  11.   My Father, arise by Your signs and wonders and visit my life, in the name of Jesus.

  12.   Like the rising of the sun, O God of wonders, arise in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  13.   Let the wonder-working power of God be released on my situation for signs and wonders, in the name of Jesus.

  14.   Let signs and wonders appear in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  15.   Holy Ghost fire, visit me with Your signs and wonders, in the name of Jesus.

  16.   Oh God, arise and hear me in the day of trouble, in the name of Jesus.

  17.   I shall not die because of my problems, in the name of Jesus.

  18.   I shall not be disgraced because of my problems, in the name of Jesus.

  19.   Let the fire of God begin to attack all miracle hijackers assigned against my life, in the name of Jesus.

  20.   God has made me a product of His possibility, no good thing shall be impossible for me, in the name of Jesus.

  21.   Every curse and covenant of impossibility over my life, break, in the name of Jesus.

  22.   Thou Goliath of impossibility in my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  23.   I shall not die undiscovered, in the name of Jesus.

  24.   I shall not die unused and unsung, in the name of Jesus.

  25.   I shall not die uncelebrated and unmissed, in the name of Jesus.

  26.   I shall not die unfruitful and unfulfilled, in the name of Jesus.

  27.   Every good thing the enemy has swallowed in my life, be vomited, in the name of Jesus.

  28.   Oh God arise and send me help from the sanctuary and strengthen me out of Zion, in Jesus’ name.

  29.   O Lord, let my soul be prevented from death, my eyes from tears and my feet from falling, in Jesus’ name.

  30.   People will hear my testimonies and glorify the name of God in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  31.   I use the blood of Jesus to fight and defeat every spirit of impossibility in my life, in the name of Jesus.

  32.   I release myself from the collective captivity of impossibility, in the name of Jesus.

  33.   Every seed, root and tentacles of impossibility in my life, die, in the name of Jesus.

  34.   I withdraw my name and all about my life from the altar of impossibility, in the name of Jesus.

  35.   I refuse to swim in the oceans of impossibility, in the name of Jesus.

  36.   Every King Uzziah that is making it impossible for me to see the glory of God, die, in the name of Jesus.

  37.   O thou that troubleth the Israel of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, the God of Elijah shall trouble you today.

  38.   Every enemy of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, scatter, in the name of Jesus.

  39.   O God, arise and uproot anything You did not plant inside the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.

  40.   Let the fire of revival fall upon Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, in the name of Jesus.
